## Summary

I have now negotiated an alliance with the Supreme Commander of the [[githyanki|Githyanki]], traveled through a crystal corridor in the deep [[underdark|Underdark]], fought druids who had allied with the wrong power, descended past a black lake with something very large living in it, climbed a rope ladder in the dark, and infiltrated a Drow noble house that has apparently been transformed into the stronghold of an Aspect of Lolth.

I also performed a Commune with Moradin that told me [[orgaz|Orgaz]] will attack again, that we are NOT facing Tiamat in [[crech-mediin|Crech Mediin]] — it is Lolth's domain here — and that Moradin can forgive me. The last answer was tentative. "Tentative" is enough. I will work with tentative.

[[elris|Elris]] is being held hostage as insurance for our completion of this mission. [[helmer|Helmer]] as well.

We are inside [[crech-mediin|Crech Mediin]] now. The Drow retreated toward their temple. We are going in after them.

## Key Events

The adult red dragon's name is [[thraxus|Thraxus]], and his rider is [[vharanis|Vharanis]] — General of the [[githyanki|Githyanki]], apparently their Supreme Commander, which in retrospect explains the quality of the soldiers we were fighting. You do not assign your best troops to protect your General's personal camp.

[[vharanis|Vharanis]] bought a conversation. The head of [[svuss|Svuss]] did that — we presented it, along with the dragon scepter that [[svuss|Svuss]] had stolen. This is what the Githyanki wanted. This is what got us an audience rather than an immediate death.

The deal: return the scepter to [[vharanis|Vharanis]], restoring his control over [[thraxus|Thraxus]]. In exchange: the [[githyanki|Githyanki]] leave [[emberstone|Emberstone]] alone, and we assist in retaking [[crech-mediin|Crech Mediin]] — the House of Webs, an ancient Drow noble house seized a month ago when three [[mind-flayers|Mind Flayers]] surprised the [[githyanki|Githyanki]] defenders. This retaking is done on behalf of [[vlaakith|Vlaakith]], the Githyanki queen, which means it is a formal state operation, which means the obligations are real.

We would get [[ryth|Ryth]] as our guide and contact. [[ryth|Ryth]] is a Githyanki dragon rider who lost control of his dragon when the scepter was stolen. He will be our guide through the [[underdark|Underdark]] and our contact with [[vharanis|Vharanis]] once we have eliminated the leadership in [[crech-mediin|Crech Mediin]]. He is competent, professional, and clearly holding something back behind those silver eyes. I respect competence. I am watching the rest.

[[elris|Elris]] and [[helmer|Helmer]] stay with [[vharanis|Vharanis]] as insurance. [[elris|Elris]] took this with reasonable composure. I intend to come back for him.

Before we left, I performed a Commune with Moradin. Three questions, three answers:

*"Will [[orgaz|Orgaz]] attack again?"* — YES. I was not surprised. I was still not prepared for the weight of it.

*"Are the Drow in [[crech-mediin|Crech Mediin]] summoning Tiamat?"* — NO. Relief and complication simultaneously. It is Lolth's territory, not Tiamat's. Different divine power. Different rules. Different danger.

*"Can I be forgiven?"* — YES. With hesitation. "Tentative" is what I read in the hesitation. I will accept tentative. A crack in stone is not the same as broken stone. It can be filled with the right mineral. It can be stronger at the break than it was before.

Moradin also added something through Divine Intervention that I did not ask for: *"Use adamantine and holy power to crush the enemy's stones. It is the most effective method."* I wrote this down exactly. I do not yet know what stones he means, but I will recognize them when I see them. Moradin does not give unclear advice. He gives advice that is not yet applicable, which is different.

The [[crystal-corridor|Crystal Corridor]] in the [[underdark|Underdark]] is exactly what it sounds like — a natural passage with crystalline walls, some kind of massive quartz formation that has grown across an ancient lava tube. The acoustic properties were extraordinary. My footsteps sounded like I was walking through a geode, which technically I was.

[[duergar|Duergar]] ambushed us there. Hostile ones, not the [[grath|Grath]] faction. We defeated them without taking significant losses, which I attribute partly to the confined geometry of the corridor favoring a shield-bearer who knows how to use a choke point.

[[ryth|Ryth]]'s informant in the [[fungal-garden|Fungal Garden]] area was a [[deep-gnome|Deep Gnome]] called [[jim-jam|Jim Jam]], living near a dried pond in a section of the [[underdark|Underdark]] that had the particular feel of a place that used to be wet and is now wrong. [[jim-jam|Jim Jam]] had turned. He had allied with the "new leadership" in [[crech-mediin|Crech Mediin]] — the Lolth loyalists. He and a group of fellow druids plus stone elementals fought us. It was serious. The elementals gave me specific trouble because fighting stone is like fighting myself at a remove — I understand how they move, but understanding does not make them less heavy.

When [[jim-jam|Jim Jam]] was defeated and questioned, he gave us information about [[lady-eovah|Lady Eovah]]. Thirty years ago she led the House of Webs. High priestess of Lolth, the kind of devotion that executes its own people for leaving Lolth's service. Lolth granted her three crystals for eternal life in service of the Spider Queen. Now her remains and those crystals have apparently been used to resurrect her as an Aspect of Lolth — a divine avatar, a partial presence of the goddess in mortal form. Drow are flocking to her. [[jim-jam|Jim Jam]] showed us the way to the Black Lake — a back entrance to [[crech-mediin|Crech Mediin]] via rope ladder. The lake has something large living in it. When tentacles erupted from the water, [[ryth|Ryth]] kicked [[jim-jam|Jim Jam]] into the lake to buy us time. We climbed the rope ladder with [[ryth|Ryth]] and escaped. After a long rest at the top, we infiltrated [[crech-mediin|Crech Mediin]] quietly with [[ryth|Ryth]] — but were quickly spotted by Drow Elite guards. We advanced inward, looting as we went. Found six scrolls: Silence, Fly, Disguise Self, Fireball, Detect Magic, Greater Restoration. A mass of Drow with a priest retreated toward their temple as we approached.

*Three crystals.* Moradin said adamantine and holy power against *stones*. Crystals are stones.

[[jim-jam|Jim Jam]] showed us the [[black-lake|Black Lake]] — a back entrance via rope ladder. The lake has something in it that is best described as: large, aquatic, and extremely unwilling to share. We went up the rope ladder very quickly. [[ryth|Ryth]] kicked [[jim-jam|Jim Jam]] into the water when the tentacles appeared, which I noted in my mental ledger under "Ryth — approach to problems." Effective. Brutal. Filed.

Long rest at the top. The stone in [[crech-mediin|Crech Mediin]] is ancient, deep-formed, the kind of stone that has been underground long enough to have entirely forgotten the sun. I feel it differently from surface stone: more patient, more dense, heavier with time. I like it, the way I like difficult mineral analyses — there is something here worth understanding.

We breached the outer halls. Quickly spotted by Drow Elite guards. Engaged. The party pushed inward. We looted as we went — six scrolls: Silence, Fly, Disguise Self, Fireball, Detect Magic, Greater Restoration. Practical. The Silence will be useful; it is a cleric's best friend in a temple complex.

From the direction of the inner temple: a mass of Drow with a priest. They saw us. They retreated into the temple.

We are going in.

- [[ryth|Ryth]] has something he is not saying. Something about a dragon called Garax, possibly. I am watching.
