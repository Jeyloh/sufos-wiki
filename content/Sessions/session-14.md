# Session 14 - Restoration and Revelation

## Summary

After Sufos's divine intervention and restoration of his stats through Moradin's blessing, we extracted ourselves from the Underdark with critical intelligence about the Tiamat conspiracy. The Githyanki general provided escort for Helmer back to Emberstone with three dragon eggs from their Queen. We crossed the desert, boarded our ship, and returned to Emberstone to find it occupied by Drow ambassadors from Kayrun. At the Academy, we ran into [[dolan-ward|Dolan Ward]]—the primary antagonist we've been pursuing—who turned out to be a former student of Copper Lord [[estian|Estian]]. He is in Emberstone, possibly visiting his old master, or possibly because he knows we are here stirring up trouble.

## Key Events

- **Restored by Moradin**: Through a divine intervention, Moradin restored the stats Sufos had "gambled away" in previous trials. Sufos swears to walk the holy path forward and reject unholy choices.

- **Loot from Crech Mediin**: Diamants (230gp), pearls (100gp), gems (200gp—Sufos holds for party distribution), **Elven Chain +1** (to Murdoch)

- **Githyanki General negotiations**:
  - General asked about Helmer's identity and how we knew him
  - We explained he's an archaeologist we met in Emberstone
  - Helmer had stolen a tool for communicating with [[vlakith|Vlakith]] (Githyanki Queen) across planes
  - Vlakith granted him an audience and sent him back with **3 dragon eggs** as a gift
  - General was ordered to escort Helmer back to Emberstone

- **Four Copper Lords confirmed**:
  - **[[zeffel|Zeffel]]** (Tiefling, House of Trading)—our contact, sent us to make peace with Githyanki
  - **[[onio|Onio]]** (High Elf, Onio's Gallery and Arts)—art dealer
  - **[[kynos-gruun|Kynos Gruun]]** (human, hardened warrior)—most powerful, collaborator with Mind Flayer
  - **[[estian|Estian]]** (human wizard, older, goatee)—Helmer's mentor, former teacher of [[dolan-ward|Dolan Ward]]

- **Scepter lore reviewed** (recalled from prior session's mind flayer letter): Three scepters exist as part of an ancient pact between a Githyanki leader and the Dragon Queen Tiamat. They can locate, summon, and dominate specifically red dragons; likely only usable by Githyanki. This *could* explain why the Githyanki broke from the Mind Flayers thousands of years ago. Kynos Gruun was in league with the Mind Flayers. Open question: is the archaeologist (Helmer?) a connection?

- **Leveled to 11**: Sufos now has the ability to upgrade his shield to level 2.

- **Athaliana killed**: Found her in the Crech Mediin prison (using True Sight), bound so her hands couldn't move. She'd been tortured by Lady Eowa for 30 years after Athaliana and her lover killed Eowa. Athaliana was a Lolth worshipper; we offered her the choice of death or enslavement to the Githyanki. Leo killed her. We took the **Ring of Sustenance** from her corpse.

- **Ryth joins the party**: The Githyanki dragon rider agreed to travel with us, but only if we'd help him reclaim his dragon [[garax|Garax]] and assist in potentially overthrowing [[kynos-gruun|Kynos Gruun]].

- **Desert crossing**: Sufos cast Find the Path to navigate; we were attacked by a large salamander with a spear—defeated it after taking damage.

- **Captain Cedric and the Falsus Vegans**: Our ship arrived; Cedric reported the gladiator festival has been running in our absence, and the ship had to circle Emberstone for days after Drow took the city. They were meeting with Kynos Gruun. We paid the crew double wages and gave an inspiring speech.

- **Sufos's personal mission**: Sufos commits to building statues of Moradin in Emberstone and on the ship—at any cost. This is his new holy charge.

- **Emberstone situation**:
  - The Kayrun/Thalia war is between humans (Thalia) and Drow/Dragon Cult (Kayrun)
  - Six Drow ambassadors from Kayrun are in Emberstone to **hire Gruun** into their war — they want the Copper Lord as a mercenary/ally
  - According to Zeffel, the deal will happen **the day after** — we must stop it
  - Assassination attempt on Gruun occurred the day before we arrived
  - **The Seekers** (mercenary band who won the Emberstone gladiator games) are now Gruun's personal bodyguards — encountered at the House of Zeffel during the trade festival
  - Iron Ring and local red-capes answer to Gruun

- **Zeffel's intelligence**:
  - Has a hidden bounty on Gruun
  - Wants Gruun eliminated before the Kayrun deal closes
  - We offer our help; Zeffel is interested

- **20,000 gp collected from Zeffel**: Payment for killing the Mind Flayer and making the Githyanki deal.
  - Sufos vaguely asked the party "don't you think we should build a shrine for Moradin?" — they agreed without thinking much about it
  - 10,000 gp → Zeffel commissioned to build a holy site/shrine for Moradin in Emberstone + smaller statue for the ship (can serve as Word of Recall destination and spread Moradin's word)
  - 5,000 gp → Leo
  - 5,000 gp → Murdoch
  - Sufos is content — Moradin is fully in his focus again

- **Meeting Helmer and Estian**:
  - Helmer is studying at the academy
  - Estian is his mentor/sponsor
  - Estian invited us to the academy the next day

- **Dolan Ward encounter at the Academy**:
  - [[estian|Estian]] introduced us to his former student — **[[dolan-ward|Dolan Ward]]** (Grim), the dragon cult leader we've fought before
  - We talked over each other's heads; Estian does not seem to know what Dolan is involved in
  - We made it obvious we knew Dolan was in the dragon cult. He looked at Estian and said, "What if I had been?"
  - Estian smiled uncertainly and excused himself for the evening
  - **Dolan cast Detect Thoughts on himself**; we tried to counterspell/dispel — he stopped us. He is far more powerful than we realized
  - He smiled, said goodbye, and left
  - **Why is he here?** Visiting his old master? Or because he knows we're in Emberstone and stirring up trouble?

## Discoveries

- **Scepter theory**: The three Tiamat scepters could explain the ancient rift between the Githyanki and Mind Flayers. They control red dragons and are likely only usable by Githyanki.

- **Dolan in Emberstone**: He is a former student of Copper Lord [[estian|Estian]]. We don't know why he's here—visiting his master, or tracking us? Estian appeared unaware of Dolan's cult involvement.

- **Vlakith and the dragon eggs**: The Githyanki Queen is personally invested in Helmer for unknown reasons. The three dragon eggs may have strategic importance.

- **Dolan's power level**: His casual use of powerful magic and ability to counter our attempts to dispel/counterspell his own spell suggests he is far beyond what we previously understood. This man is genuinely dangerous.

- **The Seekers as a problem**: These mercenaries are a direct threat to our plans to kill Gruun. Humiliating them in public might fracture Gruun's confidence in them—or it might make them more dangerous.
