# Personality

## Traits

- **Professional geologist** — snacks absentmindedly on minerals and pebbles
- **Fearless** — faces danger without hesitation, often physically throwing himself into the thick of it
- **Proud** — dwarven pride runs deep; he does not suffer insults to his craft or his people
- **Absent-minded** — can get distracted by interesting rocks or geological formations mid-conversation

## Ideals

- Serve the Earth Ashari and protect the natural world
- Honour Moradin through deeds worthy of a master craftsman
- Reclaim and restore what was lost (Pride's Call)

## Bonds

- **Moradin** — divine patron and spiritual anchor
- **Earth Ashari** — his people and purpose
- **Pride's Call** — his lost homeland, the wound that drives him
- **Edna** — his fiancée, a personal anchor in an otherwise duty-driven life
- **Azar** — his superior in the Fire Ashari, the one who directs his missions
- **The party** — Leo, Elris, Murdoch, Kael — forged together through danger

## Flaws

- Stubbornness — can be immovable once he has made up his mind
- Absent-mindedness — may miss social cues or practical details when distracted
- Pride — susceptible to insults against his craftsmanship or dwarven heritage

## Faith & Moradin

Sufos's relationship with Moradin is active and demanding. In Session 4, Moradin appeared to Sufos in a vision, clearly disappointed in some of the choices he had made. Sufos was temporarily stripped of the **diamond tooth** (later returned). This moment reinforced that his divine connection must be earned through right action, not assumed.
