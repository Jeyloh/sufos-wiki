# Backstory

## Origin

Sufos grew up in **Pride's Call**, a dwarven stronghold that was eventually lost. As a child he was evacuated during the fall of his homeland and raised in the mountains. The loss of Pride's Call left a permanent mark on him — reclaiming it is one of his deepest personal goals.

## Family

- **Parents:** (details unknown)
- **Edna:** His fiancée, met during his time with the Fire Ashari. Wedding was being planned in Vulca.

## Formative Events

Sufos discovered he had innate magical healing powers while living in the mountains after the evacuation of Pride's Call. He used these abilities to lead a group of dwarven rebels, fighting to survive and protect his kin.

At some point, he had a profound encounter with **Moradin**, the god of dwarves. This meeting gave him a divine calling and set him firmly on the path of a Cleric. One of his goals is to earn **diamond teeth** as a boon from Moradin — a mark of divine favour.

## Joining the Ashari

Sufos eventually made his way to **Dhildhor** where he joined the **Earth Ashari**, dedicating himself to their cause of protecting the natural world and the elemental planes. He later travelled to the **Fire Ashari** clan, where he trained at their blacksmithing lodge. Through dedication and skill, he rose to become a **master blacksmith**, crafting his signature molten weapons: Moltres and Molton.

## Before the Campaign

Sufos was heading to **Vulca** with his companions for a wedding — possibly his own, to Edna — when the mission changed. His superior in the Fire Ashari, **Azar**, sent an urgent Sending:

> *"Sufos, det har vært en utvikling. Trenger deg i Emberstone. En av våre agenter under dekknavnet Aradin har forsvunnet. Mer følger når du nærmer deg."*

The course was changed to Emberstone. The wedding would have to wait.
