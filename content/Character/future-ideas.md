# Future Ideas & Character Development

## Character Development

- **Diamond teeth from Moradin** — the central divine goal. What deeds will it take to earn them?
- Potential multiclass or subclass evolution as the Earth Ashari connection deepens
- Becoming a religious leader among displaced dwarves

## Plot Threads

- **Aradin** — the missing Earth Ashari agent. What did he know about Tiamat's cult in Emberstone? Is he still alive?
- **Orgaz** — the shadow demon bound to Murdoch. Sufos witnessed Moradin's disappointment partly in connection to how the party handled this. What is the right path forward?
- **Zariel's offer** — the archdevil's representative offered to free Murdoch from Orgaz. The party refused. Could this offer return?
- **Tiamat's cult** — Aradin was investigating a Tiamat cult cell in Emberstone before disappearing. What does this mean for the dragon scepter plot?

## Goals

1. **Reclaim Pride's Call** — the lost dwarven homeland. A lifelong ambition.
2. **Earn diamond teeth from Moradin** — divine recognition for a life lived rightly.
3. **Marry Edna** — return to Vulca, finish what was interrupted.
4. **Protect the party** — especially Murdoch, who carries a dangerous shadow within him.

## Theories

- The three Githyanki scepters (carved from Tiamat's wisdom teeth) may be tied to the Tiamat cult Aradin was investigating
- Kynos Gruun's alliance with the Mind Flayer may have deeper roots in Emberstone's political structure
- Pride's Call may not be fully lost — someone or something may hold it
