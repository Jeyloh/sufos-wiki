# Abilities

## Class Features

**Cleric — Divine Domain:** Earth / Nature (Earth Ashari tradition)

- Channel Divinity (multiple uses per rest)
- Divine Intervention
- Destroy Undead
- Stone-shaping or earth-themed abilities (domain specific)

## Spells

Notable spells used in the campaign:

- **Animate objects / Stone warriors** — animated stone soldiers to hold off guards in Embermines (Session 4)
- **Commune** — used to receive visions and guidance from Moradin
- Healing spells (primary role in the party)
- Area control and defensive magic

## Special Abilities

- **Moradin's Vision:** Can receive direct visions from Moradin — sometimes as blessing, sometimes as rebuke
- **Master Blacksmith:** Proficiency with smith's tools; crafted Moltres and Molton himself

## Patron / Deity

**Moradin** — The Allfather, dwarven god of creation, smithing, and the forge

Moradin's boons are earned, not given. Sufos's goal is to earn **diamond teeth** — a mark of Moradin's favour. He temporarily possessed a diamond tooth during the campaign (taken by Moradin as rebuke in Session 4, later returned).

## Custom Items

### Moltres — Molten Warhammer
A warhammer crafted by Sufos himself, infused with molten energy. Evolves through three tiers as Sufos grows in power. See [[moltres]] for full details.

### Molton — Molten Shield
A shield crafted alongside Moltres, capable of spitting lava at enemies. Evolves through three tiers. See [[molton]] for full details.
