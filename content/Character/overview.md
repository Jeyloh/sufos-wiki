## Physical Description

Stocky and sturdy even by dwarf standards. Has a distinctive set of teeth — working towards earning **diamond teeth** as a boon from Moradin. Carries the molten warhammer Moltres and shield Molton, both glowing faintly with magma energy. Tends to absentmindedly pick up and chew on interesting pebbles and minerals.
