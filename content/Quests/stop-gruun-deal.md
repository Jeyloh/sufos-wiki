# Stop the Drow / Gruun deal

## Objective

Prevent the trade deal between [[kynos-gruun|Kynos Gruun]] and the Drow ambassadors of [[kayryn|Kayrun]] from closing **today**.

## Why It's Existential (Session 15 reveal)

The deal is **the delivery channel for Drow time-manipulation magic-tech** to [[dolan-ward|Dolan Ward]]. Without that catalyst, Dolan's dragon-aging machine remains years away from operational. With it: **days** until a dragon army.

This is no longer just a political/anti-war operation. **Stopping the deal blocks Tiamat's resurrection timeline.**

## What's Known

- **Six Drow ambassadors** from Kayrun are in Emberstone
- **Deal venue:** Gruun's main office, located in his spire ([[leo|Leo]] scouted, Session 15)
- **Gruun's personal cut:** **one of the dragons** the cult will hatch
- **Bodyguards:** [[the-seekers|The Seekers]] (4 known: axe-wielding human, half-elf, dwarf, tiefling)
- **Iron Ring + Red Capes** answer to Gruun
- **[[zeffel|Zeffel]]'s hidden bounty** on Gruun is in our favor — but Zeffel must NOT be exposed
- **Assassination attempt** on Gruun yesterday (unknown actor) — Gruun is on edge

## Plan (Session 15)

[[shereen|Shereen]] flies the party (in dragon form) to the entrance of Gruun's office. Party enters the meeting and stops the deal directly.

## Risks

- 3 PCs + Shereen, no long rest, depleted spells
- Shereen does not like red dragons (relevant if anything escalates)
- Bringing Shereen in violates her stated "stay out of politics" stance — but the city itself is at stake
- [[murdoch|Murdoch]] already leaked Zeffel's involvement to Shereen
- The cliffhanger ambush by [[dolan-ward|Dolan]] + 5 at the temple may delay or prevent reaching the meeting at all

## Notes

- [[kynos-gruun|Gruun]] is **NOT a member of the cult** — he is being used as a delivery channel and bought off with a dragon
