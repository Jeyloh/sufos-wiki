# Deal with Elris's Cult in Thillion

## Objective

Resolve the situation in [[thillion|Thillion]] where a cult has formed around [[elris|Elris]], believing him to be a prophet of **Tharizdun** — the evil god of chains, shackles, and imprisonment.

## Progress

- Elris received a letter about this at the very start of Chapter 2
- No action has been taken; the party was immediately diverted to Emberstone
- The cult is reportedly "causing commotion" in Thillion

## Notes

- Tharizdun is a deeply evil deity — having an active cult believing Elris represents him is dangerous for Elris's reputation and potentially for the people involved
- Thillion is south of Emberstone — not far from the current area of operations
