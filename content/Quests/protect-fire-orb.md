# Protect the Fire Orb (Vulca / Rath)

## Objective

Defend the **Fire Orb** from the Dragon Cult — held by the Fire Ashari in [[vulca|Vulca]] within the [[rath|Rath]] area.

## Why It's Urgent

The cult **already has the Earth and Water Orbs**. The Fire Orb is their next target. [[helmer|Helmer]]'s captured cell was tasked with this orb specifically.

[[dolan-ward|Dolan]] plans to take [[rath|Rath]] within **weeks** of his dragon-aging machine becoming operational, in order to seize the Fire Orb from the Fire Ashari.

## What's at Stake

- The Fire Orb itself
- The Fire Ashari (Sufos's order, including [[azar|Azar]] and [[edna|Edna]])
- Sufos's planned wedding with [[edna|Edna]] in Vulca
- One of the last two orbs preventing Tiamat from completing her set

## Active Sub-objectives

- **Warn the Fire Ashari** — they need to know they are the next target. Send to [[azar|Azar]].
- **Block the dragon army's emergence** ([[stop-gruun-deal]], [[recover-dragon-eggs]], [[destroy-aging-machine]])
- **Secure the Fire Orb's location** — physically harden, relocate, or hide it

## Open Questions

- How heavily defended is Vulca already?
- Does the Fire Ashari have a fallback plan if Vulca falls?
- Can the orb be moved without diminishing its protection?
- How much warning does Azar need before he can mobilize?
