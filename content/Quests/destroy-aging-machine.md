# Destroy Dolan's dragon-aging machine

## Objective

Find and destroy the **dragon-aging machine** that [[dolan-ward|Dolan Ward]] is constructing to rapidly mature dragons from eggs to full combat-ready adults.

## Background

The cult has been working on this technology for a very long time:
- A **prototype** was seen by the party in the **northern mountain** they explored
- Another version was encountered during the **prequel game** (a dragon castle, played with different characters before the current timeline)

The persistent missing piece was always **time-magic**. [[dolan-ward|Dolan]] has been hunting that catalyst for years and has finally established contact with the Drow who possess it (delivered via the [[kynos-gruun|Gruun]] deal).

## How It Operates

- Egg goes in
- Drow time-tech rapidly accelerates growth
- Output: combat-ready dragon in days (vs. years naturally)
- Some output dragons are large enough to lay new eggs → exponential

## Approach Options

- **Cut the supply chain** — stop the Drow time-tech from reaching Dolan ([[stop-gruun-deal]])
- **Find and destroy the machine itself** — location unknown
- **Recover the eggs that would feed it** ([[recover-dragon-eggs]])

## Open Questions

- Where is the machine assembled?
- How portable is it?
- How many eggs has it processed already (if any)?
- Could it be sabotaged in a way that fails subtly so Dolan doesn't immediately know?
