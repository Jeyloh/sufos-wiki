# Stop Tiamat

## Objective

Prevent [[tiamat|Tiamat]], the Dragon Queen, from reclaiming control of the four elemental orbs and reshaping the world.

This is the overarching mission Moradin gave Sufos directly:
> *"The most important goal is to stop Tiamat. Never forget that."*

## The Threat

Tiamat's plan:
1. Recover the four elemental orbs (Earth, Water, Air, Fire) via the Dragon Cult
2. Gather 2+ orbs at **[[blade-garden|Blade Garden]]**
3. Use them to return the world to primordial elemental chaos — under her control

## Progress

- **Earth Orb** — Stolen from Dhildhor by the Dragon Cult. [[dolan-ward|Dolan Ward]] is connected. Location currently unknown.
- **Water Orb** — Was at risk in Three Waters. The city was defended (Ch.1 finale), but the orb's current status is unclear.
- **Air Orb** — Location unknown.
- **Fire Orb** — Sufos helped the Fire Ashari find and protect it (Ch.1 backstory).
- Sufos used **Divination** (via Moradin in Emond) to sense the exact location of all four orbs at that time — but this knowledge is now potentially outdated.

## Key Leads

- A Tiamat cult cell was active in **Emberstone** — [[aradin|Aradin]] was investigating it before disappearing
- The Dragon Cult's Chapter 1 operations (Oderman, Zuthan, Dolan Ward) were all steps toward this goal
- The three Githyanki scepters were carved from Tiamat's wisdom teeth — part of her ancient web
- Tiamat's records are **completely absent from the Crimson Assembly archives** — deliberately erased

## Notes

- This connects everything: the Earth Orb theft, Aradin's disappearance, the Emberstone cult cell, the dragon scepters, Dolan Ward
