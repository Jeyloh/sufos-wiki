# Stop Tiamat

## Objective

Prevent [[tiamat|Tiamat]], the Dragon Queen, from reclaiming control of the four elemental orbs and reshaping the world.

This is the overarching mission Moradin gave Sufos directly:
> *"The most important goal is to stop Tiamat. Never forget that."*

## The Threat

Tiamat's plan:
1. Recover the four elemental orbs (Earth, Water, Air, Fire) via the Dragon Cult
2. Gather them at **[[blade-garden|Blade Garden]]**
3. Use them to return the world to primordial elemental chaos — under her control alone

## Cult Doctrine (revealed Session 15)

- **Tiamat thrives on chaos.** The cult uses **war as a deliberate distraction** to draw attention away from the orb collection and Tiamat's resurrection.
- The current Kayrun/Thalia war is part of this strategy.

## The Vlakith–Tiamat Pact

- [[vlakith|Vlakith]] (Githyanki Queen) made a pact with Tiamat long ago, while Tiamat still walked the material plane
- Tiamat foresaw she would be sealed in a prison
- The pact: Tiamat gave the Githyanki access to red dragons → an alliance forged across millennia
- [[helmer|Helmer]] used this pact to acquire **three dragon eggs** from Vlakith

## The Dragon Army Plan

- The 3 dragon eggs will be hatched and rapidly aged via [[dolan-ward|Dolan]]'s **dragon-aging machine**
- Catalyst: **Drow time-manipulation magic-tech** (used by Drow on spider eggs)
- Without the time-tech: years to mature. With it: **days**, with some dragons large enough to lay new eggs.
- Dragon force will be used to take **[[rath|Rath]] / [[vulca|Vulca]]** within weeks → seize the **Fire Orb**
