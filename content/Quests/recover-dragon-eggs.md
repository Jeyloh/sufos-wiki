## Strategic Stakes

Each egg the cult retains becomes:
- A hatched dragon
- Aged to combat maturity in days (with Drow time-tech)
- Some large enough to lay new eggs (exponential supply)

Recovering or destroying both removes the dragon-army threat at its source.
