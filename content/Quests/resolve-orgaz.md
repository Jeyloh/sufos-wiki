# Resolve the Orgaz Situation

## Objective

Deal with the shadow demon [[orgaz|Orgaz]] bound within [[murdoch|Murdoch]]'s shadow before it causes further harm.

## Session 15 — Wish Reshaped the Past

[[murdoch|Murdoch]], drunk and pulling a card from the [[deck-of-many-things|Deck of Many Things]], cast **Wish**:

> *"I wish that Orgaz never possessed me."*

The Wish reshaped Murdoch's history:
- The Poetic Murderer no longer exists — those killings never happened
- Many of Murdoch's haunting unsolved cases simply dissolved from the record
- Murdoch is **no longer an alcoholic**
- Murdoch retains the memories of *who he was* — but the events were undone

**However:** Sufos's earlier exorcism attempt (Greater Restoration that morning) did NOT remove Orgaz. The Wish targeted possession history, not the demon itself. Whether Orgaz now has any foothold left, or returns through Murdoch's magic line, is unknown.

## Progress (historical)

- Orgaz revealed by Zariel's devil representative in Embermines (Session 4)
- Murdoch tried to Banish Orgaz — accidentally targeted himself as the same entity
- Orgaz charmed Murdoch when challenged
- Appeared to drain the soul from a dying soldier
- Zariel's offer (remove Orgaz without Murdoch losing powers) was refused
- Moradin's Commune (earlier session): "Will Orgaz attack again?" → YES
- **Session 15:** Sufos's Greater Restoration exorcism failed but restored Murdoch's full memory; later, Murdoch's Wish retroactively erased Orgaz's possession history.
