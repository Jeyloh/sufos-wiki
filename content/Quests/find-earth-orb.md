# Find the Earth Orb

## Objective

Recover the **[[earth-orb|Earth Orb]]** stolen from [[dhildhor|Dhildhor]] by agents of the [[dragon-cult|Dragon Cult]].

This is Sufos's original mission — the reason he joined the Murdoch Saints in the first place.

## Progress

- Orb stolen from Dhildhor by Dragon Cult agents (symbol identified)
- [[moltar|Moltar]] sent Sufos on pilgrimage with Moltres and Molton to retrieve it
