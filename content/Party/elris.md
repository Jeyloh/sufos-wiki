# Elris

## Description

A charismatic and bold party member. Has a following — Castor wrote a letter claiming to have been inspired by Elris and interpreted him as a prophet of the Chained God. Currently serving as a **hostage** with the Githyanki to ensure the party fulfils their deal with Vharanis.

## Relationship

Core party member. His absence as a hostage weighs on the group — the deal with [[vharanis|General Vharanis]] requires him to stay until the party eliminates the leadership of [[crech-mediin|Crech Mediin]].
