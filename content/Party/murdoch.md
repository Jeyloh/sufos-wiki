# Murdoch

Also known as **Melvin Murdoch**. His father is **Mungo Murdoch**.

## Description

A powerful magical caster who has been with the party through Chapter 1. His shadow has been behaving strangely — flickering independently — since early in Chapter 2.

## Relationship

Core party member. Sufos feels protective of Murdoch, partly because of the dangerous entity bound to him. The revelation of Orgaz was a turning point for the whole party.

## The Shadow Demon — Orgaz

Murdoch carries an ancient shadow demon named **[[orgaz|Orgaz]]** bound within his shadow. This was revealed by a devil claiming to serve [[zariel|Zariel]] (ruler of Avernus). The devil offered to free Murdoch from Orgaz without him losing his powers — Murdoch refused the deal.

Orgaz has since:
- Charmed Murdoch during a tense party moment
- Appeared to drain the soul of a dying soldier
- Continued to lurk in Murdoch's shadow

## Notable Moments

- Shadow flickering noticed by Sufos at Zeffel's after the dragon attack
- Orgaz revealed by Zariel's devil in the Embermines
- Attempted to Banish Orgaz — accidentally targeted himself as the same entity
- Refused Zariel's deal
- Won gold at the Afterlife casino
- Threw multiple Githyanki into lava with his magic
- Father Mungo Murdoch's gnome workers were freed at the slave market
