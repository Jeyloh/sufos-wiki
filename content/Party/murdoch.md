# Murdoch

Also known as **Melvin Murdoch**. His father is **Mungo Murdoch**.

## Description

A powerful magical caster who has been with the party through Chapter 1. His shadow has been behaving strangely — flickering independently — since early in Chapter 2.

## Relationship

Core party member. Sufos feels protective of Murdoch, partly because of the dangerous entity bound to him. The revelation of Orgaz was a turning point for the whole party.

## The Shadow Demon — Orgaz

Murdoch carries an ancient shadow demon named **[[orgaz|Orgaz]]** bound within his shadow. This was revealed by a devil claiming to serve [[zariel|Zariel]] (ruler of Avernus). The devil offered to free Murdoch from Orgaz without him losing his powers — Murdoch refused the deal.

Orgaz has since:
- Charmed Murdoch during a tense party moment
- Appeared to drain the soul of a dying soldier
- Continued to lurk in Murdoch's shadow

## Notable Moments

- Shadow flickering noticed by Sufos at Zeffel's after the dragon attack
- Orgaz revealed by Zariel's devil in the Embermines
- Attempted to Banish Orgaz — accidentally targeted himself as the same entity
- Refused Zariel's deal
- Won gold at the Afterlife casino
- Threw multiple Githyanki into lava with his magic
- Father Mungo Murdoch's gnome workers were freed at the slave market

## Session 15 — The Wish and Murdoch's Reshaped History

**Morning exorcism attempt by Sufos:**
- Murdoch had a bad night; [[orgaz|Orgaz]] had been active in his sleep
- Sufos sat with him, then attempted to exorcise Orgaz with **Greater Restoration**
- Sufos felt Orgaz reach out to touch him; Sufos passed a Wis save (nat 20 + 8) and channeled the spell
- **Result:** Orgaz NOT exorcised — but Murdoch's exhaustion was healed and Murdoch regained ALL the memories of people he had killed while Orgaz had control
- Murdoch, terrified, told Sufos he is afraid Orgaz is the source of his magic and isn't fully willing to let him go

**The Poetic Murderer revealed:**
- Murdoch realizes he himself was the **Poetic Murderer** — the serial killer he had spent his detective career chasing
- Orgaz had taken over and murdered through Murdoch for large parts of his life

**The Wish (Deck of Many Things, very drunk):**
- That afternoon Murdoch was standing in his room very drunk
- Said he was going to fix all the problems
- Pulled a card from his pocket and cast **Wish**: *"I wish that Orgaz never possessed me"*

**Reality reshaped:**
- The Poetic Murderer no longer exists — those murders never happened
- Many of Murdoch's haunting unsolved cases simply dissolved from history
- Murdoch is **no longer an alcoholic** — that was downstream of Orgaz too
- Murdoch remembers far fewer murders attributed to the Poetic Murderer now
- Whether Orgaz is weakened or just retroactively un-resourced is unknown

**Operational breach (later in session):**
- Murdoch accidentally let slip to [[shereen|Shereen]] that [[zeffel|Zeffel]] wants [[kynos-gruun|Gruun]] dead
- Direct violation of Zeffel's hard rule
- Containment depends on Shereen's discretion

**Combat:**
- In the warehouse fight, Murdoch took Sufos's "take this fireball" cry literally and led the assault casting **Fireball**
