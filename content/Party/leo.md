# Leo

## Description

A roguish party member with sticky fingers and a sharp tongue. Skilled at translation — can read Deep Speech / Undercommon. Uses Thieves' Cant to communicate with Aradin in prison.

## Relationship

Core party member and travelling companion. Sufos has an understanding with Leo though his habits — stealing from slavers, provoking prisoners, killing the gate guard unprompted — sometimes create complications.

## Notable Moments

- Translated the Mind Flayer's journal (Deep Speech/Undercommon)
- Communicated with [[aradin|Aradin]] in prison using Thieves' Cant
- Killed the Embermines gate guard before he finished ringing the alarm
- Woke screaming in the night when the Mind Flayer tried to reclaim its journal through him
- Drew from the Deck of Many Things and jumped to level 11, gaining a Mask of the Skull
- Could not resist stealing from a slaver at the slave market
