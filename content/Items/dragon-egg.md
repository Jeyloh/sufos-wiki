# Dragon Egg (recovered)

**One of the three dragon eggs [[vlakith|Vlakith]] gifted [[helmer|Helmer]] under the ancient Tiamat pact. Recovered by the party from Helmer's chest in the [[carls-caravan-company|CCC]] tunnels.**

## Origin

- Granted to [[helmer|Helmer]] by [[vlakith|Vlakith]], Queen of the Githyanki
- Part of three eggs delivered as fulfillment of the [[tiamat|Tiamat]]–Vlakith pact
- Intended use by the Dragon Cult: hatched, aged rapidly via Drow time-magic + Dolan's machine, raised into a dragon army
- Army's purpose: take the Fire Orb and the Air Orb from the Ashari

## Distribution of the Three Eggs

| Egg | Holder |
|-----|--------|
| 1 | **Recovered by the party** (Session 15) |
| 2 | [[dolan-ward|Dolan Ward]] |
| 3 | [[gregory-white|Gregory White]] (in Emond) |

## Strategic Value

- Denies the cult one third of their planned dragon army
- Possible bargaining chip with the Githyanki / Vlakith
- Possible study object for Sufos's order or the Fire Ashari
- Could be **hatched and raised** with proper rites — but raising a dragon is years of work
