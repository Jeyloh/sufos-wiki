# Earth Orb

**The elemental orb of Earth. Stolen from Dhildhor. Sufos's primary mission.**

## Description

One of the four elemental orbs (Earth, Water, Air, Fire) created when the primary gods — Moradin, Pelor, Corelleon, and others — captured the raw elemental chaos and bound it. Each orb guards the boundary between the Material Plane and an elemental plane. The Earth Ashari of Dhildhor were entrusted with guarding it.

The orb lives within a portal at the centre peak of Dhildhor, in a protective structure where [[moltar|Moltar]] spends most of his time.

## Current Status

**Stolen.** When Sufos returned from collecting Obsidian, Dhildhor was in chaos — corpses, blood-soaked earth. The Earth Orb had been taken. [[moltar|Moltar]] identified the thieves by a cloaked figure bearing a circle-shaped, evil-looking symbol (drawn on paper for Sufos). This is the symbol of the **[[dragon-cult|Dragon Cult]]**.

Sufos was one of three Earth Ashari sent on separate pilgrimage paths to retrieve it.

## Notes

- The Dragon Cult plans to gather 2+ orbs at **Blade Garden** to advance Tiamat's plan
- The orbs together could allow Tiamat to reshape the world by controlling the elemental planes
- Moradin used a Divination through Sufos to reveal the exact locations of all four orbs (Ch.1, Emond)
- Finding the Earth Orb is Sufos's original mission — all of Chapter 1 was shaped by this
