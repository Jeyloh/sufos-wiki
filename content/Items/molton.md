# Molton — Molten Shield

**Created by studying and draining the magic from the Lioness Shield** — a magical shield belonging to Leo's brother (who commanded the Drow ship in the Three Waters battle and was killed). The Fire Ashari blacksmiths helped reverse-engineer the magic and infuse it into Sufos's stone shield with added lava and adamantine. The Lioness Shield was emptied of its magic in the process. Pair weapon to [[moltres|Moltres]].

## Description

A heavy shield constructed from cooled lava stone and reinforced iron, with channels of slowly flowing molten rock across its face. Can project bursts of lava at enemies.

## Evolution

Molton evolves alongside Moltres through three tiers:

**Tier 1:**
- Standard shield bonus
- Can spit a small lava burst (short range, fire damage)

**Tier 2:**
- Larger lava spit (increased range and damage)
- Passive heat aura (minor fire damage to adjacent attackers)

**Tier 3:**
- Full lava eruption (cone or line, large area)
- Defensive lava pool ability

## Evolution

**Lv1: Molten Hostility I**
- +1 AC
- *Molten Hostility:* 1× per long rest as a reaction — when the wearer successfully blocks with Molten Shield (successful AC check), it can spew lava dealing 1D6 damage to an enemy within 5 feet.

**Lv2: Molten Hostility II**
- +2 AC
- *Molten Hostility:* 2× per long rest — same trigger, 2D6 damage.

**Lv3: Molten Hostility III**
- +2 AC
- *Molten Hostility:* 3× per long rest — same trigger, 2D8 damage.

## History

Inspired by and drained from the **Lioness Shield** of Leo's brother. Sufos and the Fire Ashari spent significant time reverse-engineering the magic into his stone shield, adding adamantine and lava channels. The Lioness Shield lost all its magic when the process was complete. The two weapons (Moltres and Molton) are now a matched pair.
