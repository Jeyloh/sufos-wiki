# Dragon Scepter (Githyanki)

One of three scepters carved from the wisdom teeth of **Tiamat** long ago. Part of an ancient pact between the Githyanki and the dragon queen.

## Properties

1. **Locate dragons** — especially red dragons
2. **Call nearby dragons** — summon those within range
3. **Dominate them** — control red dragons

## Notes

- Non-Githyanki cannot use it (enchantment blocks them) — as confirmed by the Mind Flayer who stole it
- The three scepters together may form a key to something larger — possibly the original pact with Tiamat
- This scepter was stolen by the albino Mind Flayer (Svuss) working with [[kynos-gruun|Kynos Gruun]]
- Recovered and now held by the party (given by [[vharanis|Vharanis]] as part of the alliance deal)

## History

The journal of the Mind Flayer described the scepter's origin and the plot to steal it. The theft destabilised [[vharanis|Vharanis]]'s dragon control and allowed [[ryth|Ryth]] to go rogue.
