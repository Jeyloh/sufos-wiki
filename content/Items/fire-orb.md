# Fire Orb

**The elemental orb of Fire. Guarded by the Fire Ashari in [[vulca|Vulca]] / [[rath|Rath]]. The Dragon Cult's next target.**

## Description

One of the four elemental orbs (Earth, Water, Air, Fire) created when the primordial gods — Moradin, Pelor, Corelleon, and others — captured the raw elemental chaos and bound it. The orb guards the boundary between the Material Plane and the Elemental Plane of Fire. The **[[fire-ashari|Fire Ashari]]** are entrusted with guarding it.

## Current Status

**Protected — but the cult's next target.**

- Sufos helped the Fire Ashari find and protect the orb during the Chapter 2 prologue
- **Session 15 (revealed by Helmer):** With Earth and Water already delivered, the **Fire Orb is the cult's next prize**. Helmer's captured cell was specifically tasked with delivering it.
- [[dolan-ward|Dolan Ward]] plans to use his rapidly-grown dragon army to **take [[rath|Rath]] / [[vulca|Vulca]] within weeks** of the dragon-aging machine becoming operational, in order to seize the orb from the Fire Ashari.
