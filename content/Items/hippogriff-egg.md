# Hippogriff Egg

**Sufos's wedding gift for Edna.**

## Description

A hippogriff egg, acquired by Sufos as the wedding gift for his fiancée [[edna|Edna]]. Noted in his journal as his solution to finding a suitable gift for the ceremony in Vulca.

## History

Sufos's journal mentions the egg was secured during his time in the Chapter 2 campaign. The wedding in Vulca remains unfinished business.
