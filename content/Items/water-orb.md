# Water Orb

**The elemental orb of Water. Originally guarded by the Water Ashari. Confirmed delivered to the Dragon Cult in Session 15.**

## Description

One of the four elemental orbs (Earth, Water, Air, Fire) created when the primordial gods — Moradin, Pelor, Corelleon, and others — captured the raw elemental chaos and bound it. The orb guards the boundary between the Material Plane and the Elemental Plane of Water. The Water Ashari were entrusted with guarding it.

## Current Status

**Stolen — delivered to the Dragon Cult.**

- Was at risk during Chapter 1 around [[three-waters|Three Waters]]; the city was defended in the Ch.1 finale, but the orb's status was unclear at the time.
- **Session 15:** Confirmed lost. [[helmer|Helmer]] stated under interrogation that *two orbs had been delivered* to the cult — Earth and Water. [[shereen|Shereen]] confirmed.

## Notes

- The Dragon Cult plans to gather all four orbs at **[[blade-garden|Blade Garden]]** to advance [[tiamat|Tiamat]]'s plan
- Together with Earth, this is the second of the four orbs the cult has secured
- Recovery would require finding wherever the cult stores its delivered orbs (likely Blade Garden, or in Disciple custody)
- Moradin's Divination (Ch.1, Emond) revealed exact locations of all four orbs at that time — likely outdated for this orb now
