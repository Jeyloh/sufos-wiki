# Ring of Sustenance

**A magic ring removed from [[athaliana|Athaliana]]'s body in [[crech-mediin|Crech Mediin]] (Session 14).**

## Description

Standard Ring of Sustenance: while worn, the wearer needs only one hour of sleep to gain the benefit of 8 hours, and consumes only one-sixth the normal food and water.

## History

- Worn by [[athaliana|Athaliana]] during her 30-year imprisonment and torture by [[lady-eowa|Lady Eowa]]
- The ring's properties may explain how Athaliana survived such an extended captivity
- Taken from her hand after [[leo|Leo]] killed her at the party's request
- Currently unattuned in party possession
