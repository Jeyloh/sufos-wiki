# Grath's Medallion

**Safe passage token through Duergar territory.**

## Description

A medallion given by [[grath|Grath]], the Duergar patrol leader, as proof that the party paid for safe passage through his territory in the Underdark (600 gp total). Acts as a "do not disturb" token for Duergar encounters in that region.

## Notes

- Whether it retains any value after the Crech Mediin mission is unclear
- Duergars respect the deal as long as the medallion is shown — within Grath's territory
