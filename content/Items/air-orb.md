# Air Orb

**The elemental orb of Air. Guarded by the Air Ashari. Location and status currently unknown.**

## Description

One of the four elemental orbs (Earth, Water, Air, Fire) created when the primordial gods — Moradin, Pelor, Corelleon, and others — captured the raw elemental chaos and bound it. The orb guards the boundary between the Material Plane and the Elemental Plane of Air. The Air Ashari are entrusted with guarding it.

## Current Status

**Unknown.** No information has been gathered about the Air Orb's current location or condition.

- Earlier intelligence: Moradin's Divination through Sufos (Ch.1, Emond) sensed the exact location of all four orbs at that time. The Air Orb was located then — but that knowledge is potentially stale.
- **Session 15 implication:** With Earth and Water already in cult hands and Fire being the next target, the **Air Orb is the cult's final target** — but the cult doesn't appear to be actively moving on it yet.
