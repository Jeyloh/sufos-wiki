# Deck of Many Things

**The legendary magical deck. Encountered at [[egdalen-warendin|Egdalén]]'s.**

## What Happened — Each Character's Draw

### Leo
- Drew a card: **advanced to Level 11**
- Received a **Mask of the Skull** (a powerful magic item)

### Murdoch
- Drew a card that gives him the ability to **change the outcome of one fateful situation** (a get-out-of-fate-free card, essentially)

### Sufos
Drew **two cards** with poor results:
1. First card: **Hexed** — afflicted with **−2 to all stats**. This curse damaged his relationship with Moradin and must be atoned for before full divine power returns. (In his journal: *"At Sufos trakk kortene fra Egdalèn skadet hans forhold til Moradin"*)
2. Second card: Showed **a head with horns** — exact effect unknown to Sufos. Something is coming.

### Elris
- Did **not draw** any cards.

## Notes

- Drawing was presented as an option at Egdalén's; Sufos's draws significantly impacted his Chapter 2 arc
- The curse (−2 all stats) is one of the reasons Sufos had to seek Moradin's forgiveness
- The "horn-head" card's effect remains unrevealed — a hanging threat
