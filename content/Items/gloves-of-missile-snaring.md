# Gloves of Missile Snaring

## Description

Magical gloves that allow the wearer to catch incoming ranged attacks.

Standard item: when hit by a ranged weapon attack, can use reaction to reduce damage by 1d10 + Dexterity modifier. If the damage is reduced to 0, the projectile is caught.

## History

Looted from the Mind Flayer's barracks in Emberstone (Session 6), along with a magical dagger, spellbook, scroll of Wither and Bloom, and gold.
