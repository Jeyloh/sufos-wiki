# Moltres — Molten Warhammer

**Originally forged by [[moltar|Moltar]], leader of the Earth Ashari.** Sufos then improved it at the Fire Ashari blacksmithing lodge by infusing it with fire and adding adamantine to the handle and head. Named in honour of Moltar. A masterwork weapon infused with molten rock and elemental fire energy. Glows faintly with magma light.

## Description

A warhammer unlike any standard-issue weapon. The head is made of cooled volcanic rock wrapped in channels of still-flowing lava, permanently fixed in place by Sufos's craft and divine blessing from Moradin. The handle and head contain adamantine reinforcement added during the Fire Ashari upgrades.

## Evolution

Three tiers with mechanical stats:

**Lv1: Overheat**
- +1 HIT / DC
- *Overheat:* Once per long rest, can overheat on command. Applies +5 flat damage to next attack, but Sufos must make a CON saving throw or drop it.

**Lv2: Pyromaniac**
- +2 HIT / DC
- *Pyromaniac:* The hammer becomes semi-sentient, obsessed with destroying rock. Every time Moltres hits rock or stone, it gains +3 to checks.

**Lv3: Lava Lash**
- +3 HIT / DC
- *Lava Lash:* Once per long rest, strike the hammer to the ground to cast Wall of Fire 5 yards in front of the struck area.

## History

Originally created by [[moltar|Moltar]] from resources Sufos had mined over weeks. Given to Sufos as his pilgrimage weapon when the Earth Orb was stolen. Later upgraded at the Fire Ashari blacksmithing lodge alongside [[molton|Molton]].
