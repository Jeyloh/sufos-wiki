# Embermines

## Description

An active mine beneath Emberstone, built over and around older Githyanki ruins. Three main tunnels lead deep into the rock. Within the mine complex is **Vulture's Vault** — the city's prison.

Access to the deep Underdark exists through a crack in a partially collapsed chamber.

## Events

- **Session 4:** Party enters seeking Aradin. Confronted by soldiers. Encounter with Zariel's devil and Orgaz revelation. Battles Umberhulks. Finds crack to Underdark — Aradin's cell is empty.
- **Session 5:** Party escapes back through mine after Underdark rest. Encounter with Bondiak on the way out.
