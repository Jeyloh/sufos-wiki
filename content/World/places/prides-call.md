# Pride's Call

## Description

Sufos's ancestral dwarven homeland. A dwarven stronghold that fell — Sufos was evacuated as a child. The name and the loss shape who Sufos is.

## Events

- **Before campaign:** Sufos evacuated during Pride's Call's fall as a child.
- His life since has been shaped by this loss; reclaiming it is a core personal goal.

## Notes

- Who or what caused the fall is unclear in current notes
- Whether Pride's Call is still occupied, destroyed, or hidden is unknown
- Sufos has never returned
