# Vulca

## Description

A city associated with the Fire Ashari, located within the broader **[[rath|Rath]]** region. The planned destination for [[edna|Edna]] and Sufos's wedding. The party was en route to Vulca when [[azar|Azar]]'s message diverted them to Emberstone.

## Events

- **Session 1:** The party was originally heading here when the mission changed.
- The wedding with Edna remains unfinished business.

## Session 15 — Vulca as Cult Target

Vulca lies within the [[rath|Rath]] area that [[dolan-ward|Dolan]] plans to seize within weeks once his dragon-aging machine is operational. The seizure is intended to give the cult access to the **Fire Orb** held by the Fire Ashari here.

**Defense of Vulca / Rath has become an active campaign objective.**
