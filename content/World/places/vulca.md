# Vulca

## Description

A city associated with the Fire Ashari, and the planned destination for [[edna|Edna]] and Sufos's wedding. The party was en route to Vulca when [[azar|Azar]]'s message diverted them to Emberstone.

## Events

- **Session 1:** The party was originally heading here when the mission changed.
