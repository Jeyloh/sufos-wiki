# Titanfall Arena

**Grand combat arena in Emberstone.**

## Description

A massive, impressive combat arena in Emberstone — disproportionately large compared to the rest of the city, with clearly enormous resources poured into its construction. Many exotic animals have been brought in.

The **Sand Festival** begins the day after the party arrived and lasts a full week. Arena fights are scheduled.
