# Blade Garden

**The Dragon Cult's convergence point for the elemental orbs.**

## Description

The location where the Dragon Cult plans to gather the elemental orbs once they have collected two or more. Mentioned during the Saints' investigation of the cult's plans inside Drageslottet.

Exact nature and location of Blade Garden is unknown — it may be a place, a site, or a ritual gathering point.

## Notes

- The cult's plan: collect two or more orbs → bring them to Blade Garden
- This advances [[tiamat|Tiamat]]'s broader plan to reclaim control of the elemental planes
- Gordon Merek's message mentioned that a warning about Three Waters passed through Blade Garden more than a week before reaching Emond — suggesting it has some kind of intelligence or communication function
- Stanley Thornewood was supposedly in Emberstone on ambassadorial business; whether this relates to Blade Garden is unclear
