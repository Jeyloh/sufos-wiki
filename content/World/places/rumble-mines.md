# Rumble Mines — Drageslottet

**Dragon Cult's Chapter 1 base. Site of the Drageslottet.**

## Description

A mine system in the Wildmount mountains, deep enough to contain an old sunken castle called **Drageslottet** (the Dragon Castle). The castle extends downward into a subterranean chamber, accessible via a hoist/elevator from within the mines.

Drageslottet was a grand, partly ruined structure with two tall towers. Occupied by the Dragon Cult and Drow forces, with Lizardfolk enslaved to the Drow. Guarded by **Dridere** — spider-drow hybrid creatures created as divine punishment in the Underdark.

## Events

- **Session 7 (Ch.1):** Saints entered the mines by stealth, found the castle top protruding from the earth, took the hoist down.
- **Session 8 (Ch.1):** Overheard [[zuthan|Zuthan]] planning the Three Waters attack. Met kobold merchant **M'Glukk** (ran the castle before the cult took over; now sells its stolen goods from a cave crack). Infiltrated the castle — killed Derreck (cultist officer) and a ritual priest. Freed a summoned devil-woman (who then attacked their enemies). Failed to defeat [[dolan-ward|Dolan Ward]] in the Great Hall — fled down a well into underwater tunnels.
- **Session 9 (Ch.1):** Met [[gordon-merek|Gordon Merek]] while sneaking through the castle. Teleported to Emond.
