# Eshian's Garden

**The estate of [[eshian|Eshian]], Copper Lord of Emberstone. Located in the [[new-heights|New Heights]] district. One of the four CCC tunnel exits.**

## Description

The personal estate of [[eshian|Eshian]] in the [[new-heights|New Heights]] district of Emberstone. The party has not entered, but identified it in Session 15 as one of the four exits of the [[carls-caravan-company|CCC]] tunnel network.

## Significance

- One of the four exits from the underground cult tunnel system
- Either Eshian is complicit, or the cult is using his estate without his knowledge — unconfirmed
- Per [[zeffel|Zeffel]]: Eshian is *neutral, easy to manipulate, but smart*
