# Rath

**A region / area associated with the Fire Ashari. Contains the city of [[vulca|Vulca]].**

## Description

Rath is the broader area in which the Fire Ashari operate. The city of [[vulca|Vulca]] is located within Rath. The Fire Ashari guard the **Fire Orb** here.

## Significance

- **Cult target (Session 15):** [[dolan-ward|Dolan Ward]] plans to take over Rath within weeks of getting his dragon-aging machine running
- The seizure of Rath would give the cult access to the Fire Ashari and their **Fire Orb**
- Sufos's wedding to [[edna|Edna]] was meant to be in [[vulca|Vulca]] (within Rath)
