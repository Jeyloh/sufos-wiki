# Astral Sea

**The planar home of the Githyanki.**

## Description

The Astral Plane — a silver void between planes of existence. The Githyanki, under their queen **Vlaaketh**, live here. They maintain planar outposts in the material world such as [[crech-mediin|Crech Mediin]].

The Githyanki fled the Mind Flayers' enslavement long ago — the three dragon scepters (carved from [[tiamat|Tiamat]]'s wisdom teeth) are believed to be part of the ancient pact that enabled this freedom.

## Notes

- [[zephyrion|Zephyrion]] (the young red dragon) mentioned dissatisfaction with "Tiamat and the Githyanki" — implying the connection between Tiamat and the Githyanki is ongoing and complex
