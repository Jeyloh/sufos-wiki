# Wildmount

**Mountain range. Sufos's childhood home region.**

## Description

A mountain province in the Tharlian Empire. Contains the ruins/ruins-in-use of **Pride's Call** (the dwarven homeland, now Kayryn territory), **Dhildhor** (Earth Ashari), and the **Rumble Mines** (Dragon Cult Chapter 1 base). Three Waters lies at its northeastern edge.

Sufos grew up in a small dwarven settlement in these mountains after the evacuation of Pride's Call.

## Events

- **Backstory:** Sufos led dwarven rebels raiding Orcs and Drow in these mountains
- **Session 7–9 (Ch.1):** Murdoch Saints explored Rumble Mines and Drageslottet here
- The **Kayryndynastiet** (Kayryn dynasty) of Drow holds Pride's Call in these mountains
