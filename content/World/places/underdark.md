# Underdark

## Description

The vast underground world beneath the surface. The party accessed it via the Embermines crack, descending through layers of caverns.

## Key Areas Visited

### Crystal Corridor
A stretch of Underdark with crystal formations. A group of hostile Duergars ambushed the party here.

### Fungal Garden
A region of large mushrooms and fungal growth. A dried-out pond sits at its centre. This is where [[jim-jam|Jim Jam]] was encountered and where [[ryth|Ryth]]'s old informant network operated.

### Lava Deep
The lowest layer reached in Session 6 — glowing lava streams visible. The first confrontation with the Githyanki happened here.

## Events

- **Session 4:** Initial descent. Duergar patrol encountered; bribed to leave.
- **Session 5:** Party retreats back up; encounters bone-grotto humanoids warning them away.
- **Session 6:** Descend to lava levels; brutal fight with Gith; Vharanis and Thraxus appear.
- **Session 7/8:** Travel through Crystal Corridor and Fungal Garden toward Crech Mediin.
