# Emond

**Capital of the Tharlian Empire. Seat of Queen Gestine.**

## Description

The great capital of Tharlia in the south. Home to the Queen's palace (**Marble Halls**), the wizarding guild **Crimson Assembly**, and the court's political machinery. A dense, complex city of competing interests and informants.

## Key Locations

- **Marble Halls** — Queen Gestine's palace. The Saints arrived here after Dolan teleported them.
- **The Sapphire Well** — Fine bathhouse where the Saints recovered after the sewer fight
- **Public Hall** — Law courts; Percy was being sentenced here when the Saints arrived
- **Lord Thornewood's villa** — Site of the Boar encounter; Stanley rescued here
- **Library** — Contains rare books including *Gods of War and Peace* (hidden room); Sufos researched Tiamat and Knights of the Eclipse here

## Events

- **Session 9 (Ch.1):** Saints teleported here from Drageslottet by Dolan Ward. Arrived in Marble Halls.
- **Session 10 (Ch.1):** Rescued Percy from court. Fought a powerful darkness creature in the sewers for a merchant wanting safe passage to Three Waters. Visited Thornewood villa — encountered Boar, rescued Stanley.
