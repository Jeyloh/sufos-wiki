# House of Bahamut

**The Bahamut section of the [[emberstone|Emberstone]] Temple of Joint Worship. [[shereen|Shereen]]'s base.**

## Description

A section within Emberstone's Temple of Joint Worship dedicated to **Bahamut** (platinum dragon god of justice). Approached by passing the row of seven god-statues (which include the vandalized Falang and Paylore figures investigated in earlier sessions).

## Layout

- Pass the seven statues of the gods to enter
- Inside the House of Bahamut: stairs leading down
- A private room downstairs where Shereen receives visitors

## Session 15

- The party debriefed [[shereen|Shereen]] in the private downstairs room
- Major lore reveals occurred here (Disciples, Vlakith pact, dragon-aging machine, orb status)
- The party left this temple at session end and saw [[dolan-ward|Dolan]] arriving with five others, scepter pointed at Shereen
