# Crech Mediin — House of Webs

## Description

An ancient Githyanki outpost deep in the Underdark, formerly known as an ancient drow noble house called **House of Webs**. Seized approximately one month ago by an unknown power using three Mind Flayers, who then handed it to a drow faction led by [[lady-eowa|Lady Eowa]].

Central feature: a temple to [[lolth|Lolth]], the Spider Queen, with a vast chasm beneath and hundreds of giant spiders hanging from the ceiling.

## Events

- **Session 7/8:** The party approached via a back entrance (discovered through Jim Jam, who was then kicked into the Black Lake by Ryth). They were spotted immediately upon entry. The drow assembly fled into the temple. The party followed.
- A massive battle occurred above the chasm, with giant spiders overhead.
- **[[lady-eowa|Lady Eowa]]** was killed.
- **[[lolth|Lolth]]** manifested and spoke with the party.
