# Carl's Caravan Company (CCC)

**A caravan company in [[emberstone|Emberstone]] used as a Dragon Cult front. Above-ground warehouse, below-ground tunnel hub.**

## Description

A caravan / shipping company in Emberstone. The party identified its warehouse area in Session 15 as a cult logistics front. Below the warehouse runs a tunnel network connecting four locations across the city.

## Layout

- **Above ground:** warehouse, guards, ostensibly normal caravan operations
- **Underground:** corridor network with cells and offices, including [[helmer|Helmer]]'s former operations room

## Tunnel Exits

The corridors lead to four points:

1. **CCC warehouse** (entry)
2. A **stone by the oasis**
3. **[[eshians-garden|Eshian's Garden]]** in the [[new-heights|New Heights]] district
4. A **smith** at the **[[house-of-copper|House of Copper]]** (smith eliminated Session 15)

## Significance

- The signature "CCC" + red five-pointed star on the note carried by [[jorek|Jorek]] led the party to this location
- Operated as a cult cell under [[helmer|Helmer]]
- Held [[edegard|Edegard]] in tortured captivity
- The chest in Helmer's office contained one of [[vlakith|Vlakith]]'s three dragon eggs

## Session 15 — Party Actions

- Bluffed past the front guard with "we know number two"
- Killed multiple cultists and a half-dragon
- Captured [[helmer|Helmer]]
- Recovered: one dragon egg, +1 Greatsword, studded leather, spell scrolls, spell ingredients, 1,800 gp
- Eliminated the cult-aligned smith at the House of Copper exit
