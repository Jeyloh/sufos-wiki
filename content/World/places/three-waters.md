# Three Waters

**Main city of Chapter 1. Where the Saints formed.**

## Description

A small town in the Wildmount Province of the Tharlian Empire, northeast of the capital Emond. Sits beside the vast **Witch Pot** lake. Mostly light timber construction on soft, swampy ground. Surrounded by marshes and bog.

Governed by young **Felix Thornewood** (inexperienced, considered unfit to lead a war defence). Part of the broader Tharlian Empire but somewhat outside its direct reach.

## Key Locations

- **Leaky Tap** — abandoned tavern, used by shape-shifting assassins in Ch.1
- **Shield of Kord** — tavern where Murdoch's cousin was found
- **Slayer's Grasp** — hunter's guild; the Saints completed an initiation here (killing a black dragon in Witch Pot)
- **Witch Pot** — the massive lake, home to monsters, site of the black dragon fight

## Events

- **Chapter 1:** Primary base of operations for Murdoch Saints
- **Session 2 (Ch.1):** Saints accidentally killed innkeeper Gorin Amurax; completed Slayer's Grasp initiation (black dragon in Witch Pot)
- **Final session (Ch.1):** Teleported back to prepare defences against Drow/cult invasion. Defeated the flying ship — commanded by **Leo's brother**, who was killed.
- **Chapter 2 start:** Sufos reunited with party here before flying toward Vulca
