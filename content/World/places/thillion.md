# Thillion

**City south of Emberstone. Site of Elris's cult problem.**

## Description

A city south of Emberstone where a cult has taken root. The cult follows [[elris|Elris]], believing him to be a prophet of **Tharizdun** — the god of chains, shackles, and imprisonment (an evil deity). Elris received a letter about this development at the start of Chapter 2.

## Notes

- Elris has followers who have gone "amok" there — causing an unspecified disturbance
- Not yet visited
