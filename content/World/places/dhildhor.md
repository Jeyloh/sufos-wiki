# Dhildhor

**Home of the Earth Ashari. Sufos's adopted home.**

## Description

A dwarven mountain town in the northern Tharlian mountains, much larger than the small settlement where Sufos grew up. Buildings and walls are built directly into the mountain, with purposely placed crystals everywhere that glow beautifully in sunlight and reflect night fires. It is a hardened, proud community — competitive builders, strong-minded people who always lend a helping hand.

The centrepiece: at a peak in the middle of Dhildhor stands a **portal**, surrounded by a protective structure and hall. Within this portal lives the **[[earth-orb|Earth Orb]]**, guarding the dimensional boundary from evil forces. [[moltar|Moltar]] spends most of his days here.

Population: no more than a hundred fully dedicated Earth Ashari. Only the most devoted servants of the earthly elements are accepted.

## Culture

- Daily mining and gathering rounds collect hardened rocks and valuable crystals
- Crystals are both decorative and magically imbued into every structure
- Town is perpetually being expanded, with focus always on protecting the orb
- Nights: gather in the main hall to drink and celebrate
- Disputes brought to Moltar and his advisors — governed as a theocracy

## Events

- **Backstory:** Sufos arrived in Dhildhor seeking allies after years as a dwarven rebel leader. Felt an immediate, strange connection — Moradin's presence. Met [[moltar|Moltar]] and joined the Earth Ashari.
- **Before Session 7 (Ch.1):** Sufos returned from Obsidian gathering to find Dhildhor in chaos — corpses, blood, the Earth Orb stolen. [[moltar|Moltar]] sent Sufos on his pilgrimage with the weapons destined to become Moltres and Molton.
