# Emberstone

![[Pasted image 20260302095311.png]]

## Description

A desert city built into and around three massive red rock cliffs cradling a central oasis. Buildings are carved into the cliff faces and constructed on top of them. Great walls encircle the city. Around the oasis at the centre: markets, tents, camels, horses, and trade caravans.

Political power is divided between the **Copper Lords** (trade lords of the Copper Ring) who effectively run the city. The city guard ([[ransell-bondiak|Ransell Bondiak]]'s Red Capes) answer to Copper Lord [[kynos-gruun|Kynos Gruun]].

Beneath the city lies the Embermines — a mine with access to the deep Underdark.
