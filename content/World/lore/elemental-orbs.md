# The Elemental Orbs — World Lore

*From "Gods of War and Peace" by Ulrich Eben — found by Sufos in a hidden room at the Emond library.*

---

## The Beginning: Elemental Chaos

From the beginning, the elements ruled all. Then came the **primordial gods** — with enough power to capture and control these forces. They bound the elemental chaos into **four orbs**:

| Orb       | Element | Guardian Clan  |                           |
| --------- | ------- | -------------- | ------------------------- |
| Earth Orb | Earth   | [[earth-ashari | Earth Ashari]] (Dhildhor) |
| Water Orb | Water   | Water Ashari   |                           |
| Air Orb   | Air     | Air Ashari     |                           |
| Fire Orb  | Fire    | [[fire-ashari  | Fire Ashari]]             |

**Obad-hai** — one of the gods' followers with unique understanding of the elements — taught the gods to use the orbs to travel between planes.

## The Agreement

Eventually the gods agreed: **none would touch the orbs**. The Ashari clans would guard them separately.

The gods became distracted — absorbed in creating their own races, followers, and territories. The elements that had once united them under chaos now sat forgotten under order.

## Tiamat's Plan

**[[tiamat|Tiamat]]** — a draconic demigod — watched from the shadows. While the gods built their divine portfolios, she tracked every Ashari guardian.

> *"Tiamat — a beast of a demigod — noted where every orb's guardian was stationed, and laid her plans. She then birthed the first dragons. The dragons would be instruments for overthrowing the Ashari and seizing control of the elements. With the elements, she would reshape the world back to what it once was — this time under her control alone. For she was the only one who had not built her power on followers to cultivate."*

## The Primordial Gods

Mentioned as the original divine powers (per Sufos's research):
- Pelor, Corelleon, **Moradin**, Yondalla, Boccob, Garl, Nerull, Gruumsh, **Lolth**

Note: Moradin and Lolth are both among the primordial gods — making the Lolth encounter in Crech Mediin carry cosmic weight.

Moradin's Divination (Ch.1, Emond library) revealed exact locations of all four orbs at that time — but this intelligence is now potentially stale.

## The Dragon Cult's Plan

Once the cult has **2+ orbs**, they plan to converge at **[[blade-garden|Blade Garden]]**. This appears to be the critical trigger point.

## The Githyanki Connection

Three **dragon scepters** were carved from Tiamat's wisdom teeth — part of an ancient pact. The current theory is that these scepters were one of the mechanisms that helped the Githyanki break free from the Mind Flayers' enslavement. The full nature of the pact is unknown.
