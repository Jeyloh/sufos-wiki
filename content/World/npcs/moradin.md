# Moradin

**The Allfather — Dwarven God of Creation and the Forge**

## Description

The chief deity of dwarves, god of the forge, crafting, and creation. Sufos's divine patron. Their relationship is demanding and direct — Moradin communicates through visions and bestows or withholds favour based on Sufos's choices.

## Relationship

Sufos's spiritual anchor and the source of his clerical power. The relationship is active and accountable — Moradin does not silently approve.

## Notes

- Sufos aims to earn **diamond teeth** as a mark of Moradin's divine favour
- In Session 4, Moradin appeared to Sufos in a vision expressing clear disappointment at choices the party had made
- Sufos was temporarily stripped of the diamond tooth as a rebuke — then earned it back during the session
- What specific acts pleased or disappointed Moradin should be tracked carefully
