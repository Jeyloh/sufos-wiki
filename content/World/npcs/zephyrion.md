# Zephyrion

**Young red dragon. Owes the party a favour.**

## Description

A young red dragon who crashed through the windows of Zeffel's establishment during the Githyanki attack. The party subdued and won him over rather than killing him. He expressed dissatisfaction with both the Githyanki and Tiamat, wanting only to be free. He promised the party a favour in exchange for being released.

## Relationship

Owes the party a favour — to be called in when needed.

## Notes

- Named by Sufos's character journal as **Zephyrion**
- He is possibly **Ryth's** dragon — in Session 13, Ryth threatened the party and demanded information about *"Garax, rød-dragen hans som vi overvant en stund tilbake"* ("his red dragon that we overcame a while back"). This may refer to Zephyrion under a different name, or a separate dragon.
- Expressed dissatisfaction with Tiamat and the Githyanki — not a loyal servant
- The Brass/Bronze dragons of Emberstone's military also appeared during this fight — they killed another red dragon outside while the party dealt with Zephyrion inside
