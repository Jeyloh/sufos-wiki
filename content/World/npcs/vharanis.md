# General Vharanis

**Githyanki Supreme Commander** (also referred to as "Rhanis" in Sufos's session notes)

## Description

The supreme commander of the Githyanki forces in the region. Commands [[thraxus|Thraxus]], an adult red dragon. An imposing and powerful figure who operates on behalf of **Vlaaketh**, his queen.

## Relationship

Tense alliance. The party bought an audience by presenting the Mind Flayer's head. A deal was struck: the dragon scepter in exchange for the party retaking [[crech-mediin|Crech Mediin]] (House of Webs). He held [[elris|Elris]] and [[helmer|Helmer]] as hostages.

## Notes

- Lost control of his dragon [[thraxus|Ryth]] after the scepter was stolen — now recovered
- His mission was to retake Crech Mediin using limited resources, on behalf of Queen Vlaaketh
- The House of Webs was seized about a month ago by an unknown power using three Mind Flayers
- The deal: party eliminates the leadership; Githyanki hold the line; Emberstone is left in peace
- Contact established via the dragon rider [[ryth|Ryth]]
