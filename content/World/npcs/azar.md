# Azar

## Description

Sufos's direct superior in the **Fire Ashari** order. Communicates primarily via **Sending** spells. Has not been met in person during the campaign.

## Relationship

Authority figure and mission controller for Sufos. Azar's orders set the entire Chapter 2 plot in motion when he diverted the party from Vulca to Emberstone.

## Notes

Sent two Sending spells in Session 1:
1. *"Sufos, det har vært en utvikling. Trenger deg i Emberstone. En av våre agenter under dekknavnet Aradin har forsvunnet. Mer følger når du nærmer deg."*
2. *"Hold lav profil. Aradin meldte om at han visste om en utvikling i Tiamats kult-celle i Emberstone før han forsvant. Aradins informasjon er viktigst."*

The second message stressed the Tiamat cult connection and told Sufos to keep a low profile.
