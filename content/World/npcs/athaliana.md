# Athaliana

## Description
A drow woman who had been imprisoned and tortured for 30 years. Scarred, weathered, bound with magic that prevented her from using her hands. A devoted follower of Lolth.

## Relationship
Enemy. She was imprisoned in the Crech Mediin fortress and we encountered her while exploring. We killed her.

## Notes

- **Crime**: Athaliana and her lover killed [[lady-eowa|Lady Eowa]]
- **Punishment**: Imprisoned by the Shadows (magical executioners who killed everyone else involved in Eowa's death). Eowa herself survived and tortured Athaliana for 30+ years
- **Lolth connection**: Devout Lolth worshipper; possessed some knowledge of [[tiamat|Tiamat]]
- **Possessions**:
  - **Ring of Sustenance** (taken from her corpse)
- **Location knowledge**: Knew of Menzoberranzan (Underdark capital) and the Abyss (Lolth's realm)
- **Fate**: When we found her in Session 14, we offered her a choice: death or enslavement to the Githyanki. Leo killed her.
