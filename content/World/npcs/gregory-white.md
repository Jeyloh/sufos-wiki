# Gregory White

**Disciple #1 of the Dragon Cult. White dragon. Senior member of the [[crimson-assembly|Crimson Assembly]] in [[emond|Emond]].**

## Description

The man known as Gregory White presents as a senior wizard of the Crimson Assembly. In truth, he is **a white dragon** — and the **#1 of the five Disciples** who lead the Dragon Cult of Tiamat.

## Relationship

Adversarial. Sufos has not met him personally, but the party has been working in opposition to his cult since Chapter 1.

## Notes

- **Disciple #1** of the Dragon Cult — the highest-ranked of the five Disciples (revealed Session 15)
- **A white dragon** (revealed Session 15) — fits the Tiamat 5-chromatic-heads pattern (white, blue, green, black, red)
- Lives in **[[emond|Emond]]**, where the Crimson Assembly is headquartered
- Senior member of the Crimson Assembly; both [[dolan-ward|Dolan Ward]] and [[gordon-merek|Gordon Merek]] work under him
- **Holds one of [[vlakith|Vlakith]]'s three dragon eggs**
- His position at the head of the Crimson Assembly means the guild's leadership and the cult's leadership are the same — likely how the Crimson archives have no records on Tiamat (purged from the inside)

## Connections

- **[[dolan-ward|Dolan Ward / Grim]]** — his lieutenant (Disciple #2)
- **[[gordon-merek|Gordon Merek]]** — Crimson Assembly agent investigating Dolan, unaware he is investigating his own boss's lieutenant
- **[[helmer|Helmer]]** — local cell leader under Dolan, captured Session 15
