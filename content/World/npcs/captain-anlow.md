# Captain Anlow

**Captain of The Strider (Tharlia)**

## Description

A stiff, formal Tharlian ship captain. Arrived via his overstyrsmann (first mate) [[overstyrsmann-lavant|Lavant]] to conduct an inspection of the captured Kayryn ship *Utherath*.

## Relationship

A brief business encounter. Paid the party 1750 gp on the spot; the remaining 7250 gp pends court processing in Tharlia (contact: Emond).

## Notes

- Ship: *The Strider*
- First mate: Lavant (a giant-like ship wizard who teleported aboard)
- Offered total bounty of 9000 gp for the Kayryn drow ship and crew
