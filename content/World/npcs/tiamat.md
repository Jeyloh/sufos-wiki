# Tiamat

**The Dragon Queen. Five-headed demigod. The overarching threat.**

## Description

The ultimate antagonist of the campaign. A draconic demigod who predates the current gods and seeks to reclaim control of the four elemental orbs — which the primary gods wrested from the elemental chaos long ago. Tiamat birthed the first dragons to serve as instruments for overthrowing the Ashari and seizing the orbs.

From *Gods of War and Peace* (by Ulrich Eben, former Archmage of Crimson Assembly):
> *"From the beginning, the elements ruled. Then came the gods — Pelor, Corelleon, Moradin, Yondalla, Boccob, Garl, Nerull, Gruumsh, Lolth — with enough power to control these elements. They captured them in four orbs: Earth, Water, Air, and Fire. Obad-hai taught the gods to use the orbs to travel between planes. Eventually, the gods agreed no one would touch them and that the Ashari clans would guard each separately. Tiamat — watching from the shadows — noted where every guardian was stationed. She birthed the first dragons as instruments to overthrow the Ashari and take control of the elements."*

## Relationship

Sufos's divine purpose — as a servant of Moradin and the Earth Ashari — is directly opposed to Tiamat. Moradin told Sufos in Session 4: *"The most important goal is to stop Tiamat. Do not ever forget that."*

## Notes

- Three Githyanki scepters were carved from Tiamat's wisdom teeth — part of an ancient pact
- Tiamat's cult is actively operating in Emberstone (Aradin was investigating a cell before disappearing)
- The dragon **Crulecs** (a colossus defeated by the Knights of the Eclipse ~70 years ago) appeared to be working for something larger — likely Tiamat
- Crimson Assembly archives contain *no records* of Tiamat or her cult — as if deliberately erased
- In Sufos's dream: he saw four orbs in a square with a hole in the centre. Moradin fighting Tiamat through it.
- The four orbs when brought together (2+ gathered at **Blade Garden**) advance her plan
