# Grath

**Duergar patrol leader. Accepted bribe for passage.**

## Description

A Duergar (dark dwarf) patrol leader encountered in the Underdark below Embermines. Accepted 600 gp from the party as payment to leave them alone and grant safe passage.

## Relationship

A transactional encounter. Gave the party a **medallion** as proof of passage through Duergar territory.

## Notes

- The party paid 600 gp total for passage for the whole group
- Gave a passage medallion to confirm the deal
