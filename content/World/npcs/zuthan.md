# Zuthan

**Drow commander. Planned the Three Waters naval assault.**

## Description

A drow leader stationed at Drageslottet who commanded the forces preparing the invasion of Three Waters. Overheard by the Saints planning the assault: a flying ship to rendezvous north of the city, three sea fleets with ballistae to attack from water, and **Dridere** (drow-spider hybrids) guarding the castle.

## Relationship

An enemy who was overheard but never directly confronted. His plans were partially foiled when the Saints freed prisoners and warned Three Waters.

## Notable Moments

- Overheard describing the Three Waters invasion plan in detail
- Mentioned **Dridere** — a spider-drow hybrid creature created as punishment in the Underdark
- One of the two present when the Saints tried to fight their way through the Great Hall (one escaped — likely Zuthan or Dolan Ward)

## Notes

- Likely escaped along with Dolan Ward when the Saints fled down the well
