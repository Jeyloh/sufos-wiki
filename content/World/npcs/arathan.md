# Arathan

**Caravan leader. Works for [[zeffel|Zeffel]].**

## Description

The leader of a trade caravan transporting goods for the Copper Lord Zeffel. The Saints rescued his caravan from an ogre attack in the desert (including a distinctive blue-tinged Ogre Mage) on the way to Emberstone. He offered silk as thanks and brought the party to Zeffel.

## Relationship

A friendly contact who served as the Saints' introduction to Zeffel and Emberstone's Copper Ring.

## Notable Moments

- His caravan was attacked by ogres including an unusual blue Ogre Mage
- Most goods were saved thanks to the Saints' intervention
- Brought the party to Zeffel; mentioned there was an upcoming auction in the city
