# Dolan Ward

**Also known as: Grim / Tiberius Glint**

The primary antagonist of Chapter 1. A wizard of the **[[crimson-assembly|Crimson Assembly]]** who turned against the Tharlian crown and allied himself with the **[[dragon-cult|Dragon Cult]]**. Operates under multiple identities.

## Aliases

- **Grim** — The mysterious cult-connected name in correspondence with "Z"
- **Tiberius Glint** — Operated a shop *Glint's Trinkets and Goods* in Three Waters
- **Dolan Ward** — True identity, exposed by **[[gordon-merek|Gordon Merek]]** (fellow Crimson Assembly agent)
- ~~**Estian**~~ — NOT an alias. [[estian|Estian]] is a separate person: Copper Lord and Dolan's former teacher

## Description

A powerful wizard who proved too strong for the Saints to defeat in direct combat (Session 8 at Drageslottet — they were forced to flee down a well). He studied at the **Amathyst Academy** (learned from Egdalén in Chapter 2).

## Relationship

Major enemy. The Saints failed to defeat him in Chapter 1. He was last seen at Drageslottet. His connection to Tiamat's cult and the Dragon Cult makes him a threat to everything Sufos cares about.

## Notes

- Summoned or worked with a black dragon that flew up to meet him as the Saints fled
- Has a ring linking him to the Crimson Assembly (identified by Murdoch)
- Teleported the Saints to Emond (possibly inadvertently helping them escape)
- Involved in a court conspiracy — Gordon Merek suspects someone in the Tharlian court is collaborating with him
- Last known not to have been in Emond for over a month (per Gordon's message)
- The Crimson Assembly's archives contain no records of Tiamat or her cult — as if erased

**Location**: Emberstone — encountered at the Academy

**Connection to Estian**: Dolan is a former student of Copper Lord [[estian|Estian]]. Estian introduced Dolan to us. Estian does NOT appear to know about Dolan's dragon cult involvement.
