# Solona

**Fire Ashari lieutenant. Azar's second-in-command (alongside Kai).**

## Description

One of Azar's lieutenants in the Fire Ashari. Together with [[kai|Kai]], befriended Sufos during his novice period and helped him integrate into Fire Ashari culture. Introduced him to the blacksmithing lodge.

## Relationship

A trusted friend and colleague from Sufos's Fire Ashari years.

## Notes

- Went with Sufos and Kai to hunt the artifact to hide the Fire Orb
- Introduced Sufos to the blacksmithing lodge, which became a major part of his identity
