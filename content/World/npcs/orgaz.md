# Orgaz

**Ancient Shadow Demon**

## Description

An ancient shadow demon bound within [[murdoch|Murdoch]]'s shadow. Its presence was revealed by a devil serving [[zariel|Zariel]] in the Embermines. When the torches were lit, Orgaz appeared as a massive shadow behind Murdoch, larger and more defined than it should be.

## Relationship

A hidden threat to the party. Sufos is aware of Orgaz and concerned for Murdoch. Moradin's vision in Session 4 was partly related to how the party handled this situation.

## Notes

- Charmed Murdoch when he tried to Banish it — Murdoch accidentally included himself as the same target
- Appeared to drain the soul from a dying soldier
- Zariel's devil offered to remove Orgaz from Murdoch without him losing his powers — Murdoch refused
- The offer may still stand
- Unknown how long Orgaz has been present or how deep the bond goes
