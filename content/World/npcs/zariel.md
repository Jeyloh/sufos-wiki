# Zariel

**Archduchess of Avernus — First Layer of Hell**

## Description

The ruler of Avernus, the first layer of the Nine Hells. A powerful archdevil who has taken an interest in [[murdoch|Murdoch]]'s situation — specifically the shadow demon [[orgaz|Orgaz]] bound to him.

## Relationship

Has not appeared directly. A devil representative (a glaskie devil with wings and horns) appeared in the Embermines claiming to speak for Zariel. The offer made was genuine enough to demonstrate knowledge of Orgaz.

## Notes

- The representative offered to remove Orgaz from Murdoch without him losing his powers
- Murdoch refused the deal
- The representative implied the offer remains open
- Why Zariel cares about Orgaz or Murdoch is unknown
