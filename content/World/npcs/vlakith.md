# Vlakith

**Queen of the Githyanki. Maker of the ancient pact with [[tiamat|Tiamat]].**

## Description

The ruler of the Githyanki, residing on the Astral Plane. The party has not met her directly — only through her actions and her general's reports.

## Relationship

Indirect. Her decisions shape the dragon politics that the party is now caught in.

## Notes

- **The Vlakith–Tiamat Pact (Session 15 reveal):**
  - Made long ago, while [[tiamat|Tiamat]] still walked the material plane
  - Tiamat foresaw she would be sealed in a prison
  - The pact gave the Githyanki access to red dragons, forging an alliance
  - This pact is one of the mechanisms that helped the Githyanki break free from the Mind Flayers
- **Helmer used this pact** to obtain three dragon eggs from her
- She granted [[helmer|Helmer]] an audience across planes after he stole the communication tool
- Sent Helmer back to Emberstone with the three dragon eggs as gift / pact-fulfillment
- Her general was ordered to escort Helmer back
