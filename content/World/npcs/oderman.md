# Oderman

**Dragon Cult leader. Occupied Lord Therris's villa.**

## Description

A disciple and local leader of the Dragon Cult operating in Wildmount. Took over Lord Therris's villa with the kenkuer leader Ozniak, using it as a staging area. Refused to talk under pressure and was killed by the Saints.

## Notable Moments

- Held Lord Therris's villa with Ozniak and kenkuer forces
- Killed Lord Therris in a ritual (found in the cellar, legless)
- Carried a letter found on his body: a war between good and evil to begin in **"the eighth month"**
- The Dragon Cult emblem on one of Oderman's daggers was what Sufos recognised — confirming this was the same group that stole the [[earth-orb|Earth Orb]] from Dhildhor
- Killed by the Saints; **Ozniak escaped** into Rumble Mines

## Notes

- His associate **Derreck** (a cultist officer) was later killed inside Drageslottet
