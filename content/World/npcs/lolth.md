# Lolth

**The Spider Queen — Drow Goddess**

## Description

The goddess of the drow, spiders, and the Underdark. The temple of Lolth in Crech Mediin / House of Webs was the site of the final confrontation in Chapter 2. An epic battle with hundreds of giant spiders overhead and combat above a deep chasm.

## Relationship

An entity far beyond the party's normal scope. She manifested and spoke with them directly after [[lady-eowa|Lady Eowa]] was slain. The content of that conversation and what it means going forward is unknown.

## Notes

- Her temple in House of Webs was the destination of a drow ritual when the party arrived
- Hundreds of giant spiders hang from the temple ceiling
- A drow assembly retreated to her temple just before the party followed
- **Lady Eowa** was killed in the ensuing battle
- Lolth then spoke to the surviving party members
