# Kai

**Fire Ashari lieutenant. Azar's second-in-command.**

## Description

One of Azar's lieutenants in the Fire Ashari clan. Befriended Sufos when he joined the Fire Ashari as a novice. Joined Sufos on a hunt for an artifact to help hide the Fire Orb — a mission that earned Sufos Azar's respect.

## Relationship

A trusted friend from Sufos's time with the Fire Ashari. One of the people who taught Sufos Fire Ashari culture and introduced him to the blacksmithing lodge.

## Notes

- Together with [[solona|Solona]], helped Sufos integrate into the Fire Ashari
- Went on the Fire Orb artifact hunt together with Sufos
