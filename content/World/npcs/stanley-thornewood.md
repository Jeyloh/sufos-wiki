# Stanley Thornewood

**Ambassador. Member of Crimson Assembly. Son of Walter Thornewood.**

## Description

The elder son of old lord **Walter Thornewood**. A member of the **Crimson Assembly** and an ambassador. Was believed to be in Emberstone on an ambassadorial mission, but was actually captured and held in his father's Emond villa by **Boar** (a half-orc assassin). His wife was killed; his young son survived.

## Relationship

Rescued by the Saints in Chapter 1. Deeply grateful. Promised to do what he could in the Tharlian court, though he needed time to grieve and recover.

## Notes

- Was being used as leverage in a plot to frame drow for killing the Queen's son (using Scroll of Domination on captured drow)
- Gave the Saints the **teleport key runes** to travel back to Three Waters
- Said: "Someone will be held accountable for this"
- His father **Walter Thornewood**'s whereabouts are unknown — he was not in his own villa
- His younger brother **Felix Thornewood** leads Three Waters
