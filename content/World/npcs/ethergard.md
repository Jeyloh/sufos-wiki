# Ethergard

**Red Dragonborn. Bahamut's priest in Emberstone.**

## Description

A red dragonborn who serves as Bahamut's priest in Emberstone. The guard Sherrin / [[cheereen|Cheereen]] mentioned that the vandalism on the city's divine statues should be reported to Ethergard.

## Relationship

Not yet met directly. Potentially a useful contact for divine matters and the Tiamat cult investigation.

## Notes

- The vandalized statues were of Falang (god of travel) and Paylore (god of the sun)
- Vandalism suspected to be the work of Tiamat cultists
- Ethergard may have knowledge of Tiamat cult activity in the city
