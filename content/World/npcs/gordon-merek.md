# Gordon Merek

**Crimson Assembly agent. Court spy investigating Dolan Ward.**

## Description

An invisible agent the Saints discovered hiding in Drageslottet. Works for the Crimson Assembly and the Tharlian crown, investigating Dolan Ward (Grim/Tiberius). Claims to also work under **Gregory White**. Sent coded messages that disappeared after reading.

## Relationship

An ally — wary but useful. Gave the Saints a walkie-talkie-style communication device, teleported them to Emond's Marble Halls, and sent a long letter with political intelligence. Sufos trusts him cautiously.

## Notes

- Suspected someone in the Tharlian court is part of the conspiracy (possibly Lord Gregory, but not confirmed)
- Reported that Dolan had not been in Emond for over a month
- Found no records of Tiamat or her cult in Crimson's own archives — as if deliberately erased
- Shared intel about the Thornewood family
- Remained in Emond to influence events there while the Saints returned to Three Waters
