# Kynos Gruun

**Copper Lord of Emberstone**

## Description

A hardened warrior; the most powerful of Emberstone's four Copper Lords. Wears white clothing and a turban; carries a saber on his back; has a black beard. Stern, commanding presence. He commands the **Red Capes** (the city guard) and controls the **Iron Ring** (criminal organization).

## Relationship

Presented himself as a neutral power broker, but the Mind Flayer's journal identified him as a **collaborator** with the Illithid. [[zeffel|Zeffel]] wants him deposed. The party is positioned against him without his knowledge.

## Notes

- Collaborating with the Mind Flayer (Svuss) according to the Illithid's own notes (since eliminated)
- **Zeffel's target**: Zeffel has a hidden bounty on Gruun and wants him dead before any deal with Kayrun is closed
- **Recent bodyguards**: Just hired **[[the-seekers|The Seekers]]** (mercenary band that won the Emberstone gladiator games) as his personal security
- Commands the Red Capes through Ransell Bondiak
- Controls the Iron Ring (criminal organization)

## Session 14 Encounter

**Drow ambassadors from Kayrun**: Six Drow ambassadors are in Emberstone to **hire Gruun** into the Kayrun/Thalia war. The war is between humans (Thalia) and Drow/Dragon Cult (Kayrun). They want Gruun as a mercenary/ally. According to Zeffel, the deal will happen **the day after** our arrival. This must be stopped.

**Direct interaction**: At the **House of Zeffel** (during the trade festival, everyone gathered there), we loudly mocked [[the-seekers|The Seekers]], saying they "only won the kindergarten games in the gladiator arena versus prisoners because we were out saving the city." Gruun came over and bought us drinks. He seemed annoyed that we had been absent and expressed confusion about how we had defeated the Githyanki and Mind Flayer while his mercenaries were playing in the arena.

## Connection to Dolan

**Unknown direct relationship**: Dolan Ward (dragon cult leader) is a former student of Copper Lord [[eshian|Eshian]] and was encountered in Emberstone during Session 14. Dolan and Eshian are separate people.

## Session 15 Update — Gruun's Real Role

**Gruun is NOT a member of the Dragon Cult.** Confirmed by [[helmer|Helmer]]'s interrogation. He is a Mind Flayer collaborator (already known) but he is not a Disciple.

**He IS the cult's delivery channel for Drow time-magic.** Dolan needs the Drow time-manipulation tech to make his dragon-aging machine viable. Gruun is being courted by the Drow ambassadors as a mercenary alliance — and the trade carries the time-tech with it.

**Gruun's personal cut: a dragon.** The Drow promised him **one of the dragons** that the cult will hatch and grow. That, more than gold, is what makes him hard to pull off the table.

**The Gruun deal stop is therefore existential.** Without the time-tech reaching Dolan, the dragon-army timeline goes from days back to years. The political reasons to stop the deal already existed. The cult-blocking reason now dwarfs them.

**Leo's overnight scout (Session 15):** Located Gruun's main office at the spire — likely venue for the Drow meeting today.

**Plan (Session 15):** [[shereen|Shereen]] flies the party (in dragon form) directly to the entrance of Gruun's office to interrupt the meeting.
