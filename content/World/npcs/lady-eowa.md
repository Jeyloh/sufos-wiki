# Lady Eovah

**Ancient drow High Priestess of Lolth. Resurrected as an Aspect of Lolth.**

## Description

30 years ago, Lady Eovah was the leader of **House of Webs** and one of Lolth's most trusted High Priestesses. She ruthlessly purged drow who conspired to reach the surface. As reward, Lolth granted her **three crystals** conferring eternal life, binding her to serve the Spider Queen forever.

She was treated as an **"aspect" of Lolth** — a Jesus-like figure in drow religion. Her remains and the three crystals were recently used to bring her back. Demonic figures and drow were observed flocking to House of Webs in the lead-up to her resurrection.

The party fought and killed the resurrected Eovah in Lolth's temple by destroying **three stones in three portals** — the mechanism of her resurrection.

## Relationship

An enemy. Eliminated as the primary target of the Crech Mediin mission.

## Notes

- The three crystals tied to her eternal life were central to the resurrection ritual
