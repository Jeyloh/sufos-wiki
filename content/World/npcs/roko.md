# Roko

**Kenkuer leader. Helped the Saints against Oderman.**

## Description

The leader of a kenkuer group who chose cooperation over conflict with the Saints. Revealed that his people had been dragged into the Dragon Cult's plans against their will under the thumb of Ozniak — their own corrupt leader.

## Relationship

An unlikely ally in Chapter 1. Provided intelligence about Oderman and helped reduce resistance at Lord Therris's villa by clearing out kenkuers loyal to Ozniak.

## Notable Moments

- Told the Saints about the collaboration between the Dragon Cult and the kenkuer leader **Ozniak**
- Helped clear kenkuers from Lord Therris's villa so the Saints could break in
- His people wanted to flee the area and live freely away from the cult

## Notes

- Also written as "Rocco" in some journal entries
- **Ozniak** — the kenkuer leader working with Oderman — escaped into Rumble Mines
