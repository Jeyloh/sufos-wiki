# Helmer

**Local Dragon Cult cell leader in [[emberstone|Emberstone]]. Posed as an archaeologist studying under [[eshian|Eshian]]. Captured by the party in Session 15.**

## Description

For most of his appearances Helmer presented as an archaeologist — first met at Zeffel's establishment during the Gith attack (Session 3), where he spoke Githyanki and helped calm the warriors. He went on to study under [[eshian|Eshian]] at the Academy and was later "escorted back" from the Githyanki plane by their general, ostensibly with three dragon eggs as a gift.

In Session 15, the party discovered the truth: Helmer is the **leader of the local Dragon Cult cell** in Emberstone. The "innocent archaeologist" was the cell commander all along.

## Relationship

Hostile. Currently captured alive in the [[carls-caravan-company|CCC]] tunnel network. Witness, prisoner, potential intelligence asset.
