# Estian

**Copper Lord of Emberstone. Wizard. Academy mentor.**

## Description

An older wizard with a grey goatee and fine robes. One of Emberstone's four Copper Lords — occupies the scholarly and arcane wing of the Copper Ring's power. Associated with the Academy in Emberstone.

## Relationship

Met briefly at the Academy during Session 14, introduced through [[helmer|Helmer]], who studies under him. Part of [[zeffel|Zeffel]]'s wider circle of influence in Emberstone. Appears to view the party with cautious neutrality — he is not hostile, but he is not an ally.

## Connections

- **[[helmer|Helmer]]** — Current protégé and student at the Academy
- **[[dolan-ward|Dolan Ward]]** — Former student; now a dragon cult operative. Estian appears unaware of Dolan's cult involvement
- **[[zeffel|Zeffel]]** — Part of the same Copper Ring power structure

## Notes

- Invited the party to meet his former student (Dolan) at the Academy during the trade festival
- When the party made it clear they suspected Dolan of dragon cult ties, Estian smiled uncertainly and excused himself — suggesting discomfort rather than knowledge
- Does NOT appear to know about Dolan's cult involvement
- His association with both Helmer and Dolan Ward (separately) makes him a potentially important figure as the Tiamat plot develops
