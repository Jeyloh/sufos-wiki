# Moltar

**Leader of the Earth Ashari in Dhildhor. Sufos's mentor.**

## Description

A dwarven Cleric of the Earth Domain — the same path as Sufos. Moltar's sacred duty from Moradin is to protect the Earth Ashari and their **Earth Orb**. Runs Dhildhor as a theocracy, solving disputes by seeking answers from Moradin. Stern but wise.

## Relationship

The most important mentor figure in Sufos's life. Moltar convinced Sufos to join the Earth Ashari, then personally mentored him. When the Earth Orb was stolen, Moltar was in distress — and it was Moltar who gave Sufos his iconic weapons.

## Notable Moments

- Convinced Sufos to join the Earth Ashari after Sufos arrived in Dhildhor seeking allies
- Gifted Sufos the stone hammer (later named **[[moltres|Moltres]]**) and shield (later **[[molton|Molton]]**) when the orb was stolen: *"This was supposed to help you in your pilgrimage. They are created by resources I've asked you to mine for weeks. Now, you must take these crafts and find the stolen orb. Do this, as part of your pilgrimage."*
- Both Moltres and Molton are named in his honour (Molt-res / Molt-on)
- Was visibly distressed and with his advisors when Sufos returned to find Dhildhor in chaos

## Notes

- Guardian of the **Earth Orb** and the portal at Dhildhor's centre peak
- After Chapter 1's events, Moltar advised Sufos to continue his pilgrimage to the Fire Ashari rather than remain reactive
- Sufos reported back to him after the failure to retrieve the Earth Orb
