# Jorek

**A slave boy in Emberstone. Acted as courier for a posthumous note from a dead half-elf gladiator.**

## Description

Young, presented as a slave. Approached Sufos in the street to deliver a folded note.

## Relationship

Encountered briefly. Performed his task and was not interrogated further.
