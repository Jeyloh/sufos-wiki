# Leo's Brother

*True name unknown.*

## Description

Leo's brother, who had apparently sided with — or was serving — the Drow forces aligned with the Dragon Cult. He commanded the **flying Drow warship** sent to lead the assault on Three Waters in the Chapter 1 finale.

## Relationship

An enemy in the field, but Leo's family. The weight of killing him (or being present when it happened) is part of the party's history.

## Notable Moments

- Commanded the Drow flying ship in the Three Waters invasion
- Killed in the battle
- Had a magical shield called the **Lioness Shield** — after his death, Sufos and the Fire Ashari studied it and used its magic as the basis for [[molton|Molton]]. The Lioness Shield was drained empty in the process.

## Notes

- The drow noted Leo by name during an encounter, suggesting his brother's affiliation with them was known on their side
- Why he was with the Drow forces is unexplained — coercion, allegiance, or circumstance?
