# Aradin

A **Fire Ashari** agent (officer/operative under [[azar|Azar]]) operating under a cover identity in [[emberstone|Emberstone]]. His disappearance was the inciting event for Chapter 2. **Killed in the Emberstone arena before the party could free him** — but his last act guided the party to the cult cell he had been investigating.

## Description

A half-elf Fire Ashari undercover agent. Sufos's leader [[azar|Azar]] sent the party to find him.

## What He Knew

Aradin discovered a **Tiamat cult cell** operating in Emberstone before he was caught and imprisoned. Azar's message stressed that his information was critical. **Session 15:** the cell he had been investigating turned out to be [[helmer|Helmer]]'s — operating out of [[carls-caravan-company|Carl's Caravan Company]] (CCC).

## Notable Moments

- Sent to Emberstone as an undercover Ashari agent
- Was framed for theft (at [[onio|Onio]]'s gallery) and imprisoned in **Vulture's Vault** (Embermines prison)
- **The party met him in prison** and planned with him — hinted via Thieves' Cant that a collapsed cell near a mine cave-in was relevant
- Could not be released
- Was moved out of the prison and forced into the **arena fights** as part of the Emberstone festival
- **Killed in the arena** in the days before the party returned from Crech Mediin

## Session 15 — Confirmation of Death

[[azar|Azar]] confirmed Aradin's death via Sending in Session 15:
> *"Synd med Aradins død."* ("Sad about Aradin's death.")

## Session 15 — His Last Message (the CCC letter)

**Aradin sent the CCC letter himself, before the arena killed him.** Slave boy [[jorek|Jorek]] delivered it to Sufos in the street. Contents:

> *"Asken glemmes ei"* — signed **CCC** + a **red five-pointed star**

Notes on the contents (what is fact vs. what is theory):
- *"Asken glemmes ei"* — meaning unknown. Sufos noted the phonetic similarity between *asken* (ash) and *Ashari* and theorized it might be an authentication phrase, but **Azar does not recognize it**, so the Ashari-code interpretation is unconfirmed speculation.
- **CCC** — confirmed to be **[[carls-caravan-company|Carl's Caravan Company]]**, the cult cell location Aradin had been investigating. The letter functioned as an address.
- **Red five-pointed star** — matched the cult symbol on the chest inside the warehouse.
- Azar didn't recognize CCC / the star — Aradin appears to have built his own field cipher for the Emberstone op.

**His mission succeeded posthumously.** The party would not have found Helmer's cell without Aradin's last note.
