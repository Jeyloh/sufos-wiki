# Aradin

A **Fire Ashari** agent (officer/operative under [[azar|Azar]]) operating under a cover identity in Emberstone. His disappearance is the inciting event for Chapter 2.

## Description

A Fire Ashari undercover agent. Sufos's leader [[azar|Azar]] sent the party to find him. He was imprisoned in **Vulture's Vault** (Embermines prison), framed for a theft at Onyo's gallery. He was not in his cell when the party checked — he had been moved.

## Relationship

Not personally known to Sufos before the campaign. Sufos's mission is to find him and recover whatever intelligence he had.

## What He Knew

Aradin discovered a **Tiamat cult cell** operating in Emberstone before he disappeared. Azar's message stressed that his information is critical.

## Notable Moments

- Sent to Emberstone as an undercover Ashari agent
- Was framed for theft and imprisoned in Vulture's Vault
- Spoke briefly with Leo and the party in prison using **Thieves' Cant**: hinted that a collapsed cell near a mine cave-in was relevant
- Had already been moved from his cell by the time the party broke into the mine
- Current location unknown

## Session 15 Update — Aradin Confirmed Dead

[[azar|Azar]] confirmed Aradin's death via Sending in Session 15:
> *"Synd med Aradins død."* ("Sad about Aradin's death.")

The Tiamat cult intelligence Aradin gathered before his death has not been recovered. His informant network and any drop points he established remain unknown.

**The CCC letter** Sufos received (signed CCC + red five-pointed star, with the Ashari authentication phrase *"Asken glemmes ei"*) was therefore **NOT from Aradin** — he was already dead. Identity of the sender unknown. They knew Ashari sign-language; Azar himself does not recognize the CCC + star sigil.
