# Overstyrsmann Lavant

**First mate and ship wizard of The Strider.**

## Description

A massive, giant-like figure who serves as the first mate (*overstyrsmann*) and ship wizard aboard [[captain-anlow|Captain Anlow]]'s vessel *The Strider*. He teleported directly onto the Saints' ship to deliver the inspection request from Anlow — a dramatic entrance.

## Relationship

A brief, businesslike encounter. He represented Anlow's authority when the Tharlian ship came to inspect the captured Drow vessel.

## Notes

- Also spelled "Levant" in some journal entries
- His teleportation ability suggests significant magical power for a ship officer
