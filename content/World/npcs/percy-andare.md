# Percy Andare

**Guide and sometime-companion of the Saints.**

## Description

A scruffy, drug-using guide ("knarkeren") hired by Uria Zar to lead the Saints through the Arazun mountains. Proved reliable despite his habits. Eventually became a loose associate of the Saints.

## Relationship

A friendly recurring figure from Chapter 1. Was arrested and facing court sentencing in Emond when the Saints arrived — Murdoch paid his fine to have him released.

## Notable Moments

- Guided the Saints through Arazun mountains; they encountered Kayryn spies along the way
- Helped the Saints escape Uria Zar's ambush on the return route
- Found himself in court in Emond facing a two-week prison sentence (Murdoch paid a fine to free him)
- Was with the Saints during the Emond sequence (Ch.1 Sessions 9–10)
