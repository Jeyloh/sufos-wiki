# Kaptein Cedric Krast

**Captain of the Air Paragons vessel.**

## Description

A half-orc with a distinctive curly moustache. Experienced sea captain making his first voyage as an airship commander — he is candid about this. Sufos questioned his qualifications early on; Cedric has extensive nautical experience but airships are new territory.

## Relationship

The captain of the party's transport at the start of Chapter 2. They named the ship **"Falsus' Vengeance"** (though Elris failed his Int check and wrote "Falsus' Vegans" on the hull).

## Notable Moments

- Hired as captain of the Air Paragons vessel heading for Vulca
- Sufos openly questioned his qualifications — first airship command
- A new crew member, [[kael|Kael]], joined under him as scout
- The ship was attacked by a Kayryn warship; the party repelled the attack
