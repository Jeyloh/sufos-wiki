# Onio

**Copper Lord. Art dealer.**

## Description

One of Emberstone's four Copper Lords. Distinguished by his black hair and fine clothes. Deals in art and artefacts. Has his own gallery in the city.

## Relationship

Not yet directly engaged with. Part of the Copper Ring power structure.
