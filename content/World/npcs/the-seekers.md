# The Seekers

## Description
A band of mercenaries who won the Emberstone Trade Festival Gladiator Games (while we were in the Underdark). They are currently employed by [[kynos-gruun|Kynos Gruun]] as his personal bodyguards.

## Background

- **Gladiator victory**: Defeated all competitors in the Emberstone arena
- **Recent hire**: Just recently brought on as Gruun's security force
- **Combat proven**: They won the most prestigious martial competition in the city

## Relationship

Adversarial. We plan to kill them—not out of direct conflict, but because they are protecting our target [[kynos-gruun|Kynos Gruun]]. Removing them will make assassinating Gruun easier.

## Session 14 Encounter

At the **House of Zeffel** (during the trade festival), we deliberately mocked them, saying they "only won the kindergarten games in the gladiator arena versus prisoners because we were out saving the city."

**Why we did this**:
- Provoke them into being distracted or angry
- Weaken their morale and confidence
- Make them easier to kill / easier targets when we move against Gruun

## Strategic Value

- **Bodyguard removal**: If we can take out The Seekers, Gruun loses his protection layer
- **Morale damage**: Public humiliation might fracture team cohesion
- **Risk**: They might become more aggressive and dangerous as a result
