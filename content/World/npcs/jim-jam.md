# Jim Jam

**Deep Gnome, former informant**

## Description

A Deep Gnome who was one of [[ryth|Ryth]]'s informants in the Underdark, stationed near a dried-out pond in the Fungal Garden. Knew an alternate route into [[crech-mediin|House of Webs]].

## Relationship

Betrayed Ryth and sided with the new leadership in the Underdark. Taken prisoner to guide the party to the House of Webs. Kicked into a creature-infested pool by Ryth. Fate unknown.

## Notes

- Was accompanied by druids and two stone elementals when he turned hostile
- Fought the party at the Fungal Garden
- Taken prisoner as a guide
- Ryth kicked him into the water (where Sufos's Commune had detected a lurking creature) as the party escaped
