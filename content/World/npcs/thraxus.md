# Thraxus

Adult red dragon, mount of [[vharanis|General Vharanis]].

## Description

A large adult red dragon who serves Vharanis as a warbeast and mount. Appeared dramatically from the darkness of the Underdark lava levels alongside the Githyanki Supreme Commander.

## Relationship

Encountered as part of the Githyanki alliance. Not personally engaged with by Sufos.

## Notes

- Controlled by Vharanis via the dragon scepter (or by other means once the scepter was recovered)
- Distinct from [[ryth|Ryth]], the dragon whose rider is the party's contact
