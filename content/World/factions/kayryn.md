# Kayryn (Kayryndynastiet)

**The Drow Empire. Occupiers of Pride's Call.**

## Description

The Drow empire that formed when the dwarves were driven from **Pride's Call** during the 100-year war. Kayryn now occupies what was the dwarven homeland under the Wildmount mountains. Trade and travel with Kayryn is banned by Tharlia, and diplomatic relations are frozen.

They maintain aerial naval power (warships) and have been expanding into areas around Emberstone and Three Waters.

## Relationship

Sufos's people — the dwarves — were displaced by this empire. **Pride's Call** is under their control. Hostile by history and personal stakes.

## Notable Events

- **Ch.1:** Kayryn spies (Duergars, bugbears, drow) were discovered building a watchtower in the Arazun mountains
- **Ch.1:** Zuthan (drow commander) orchestrated the attack on Three Waters with flying ship + three sea fleets
- **Ch.2:** Kayryn warships patrol the airspace around Emberstone; the party was attacked by one in Session 1
- Ambassadors from Kayryn visited Emberstone to inspect the Iron Ring mercenaries
