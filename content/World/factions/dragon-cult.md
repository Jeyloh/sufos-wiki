# Dragon Cult — Followers of Tiamat

**The overarching antagonist faction. Worshippers of Tiamat.**

## Description

A secretive cult dedicated to [[tiamat|Tiamat]], the Dragon Queen. They are assembling the four elemental orbs to further her plan to reclaim control of the elements and reshape the world. Sufos identified their emblem — a dragon-related symbol — on a dagger at Lord Therris's villa.

## Structure — The Five Disciples (Session 15 reveal)

The cult is led by **five Disciples**. Likely mapping to the five chromatic dragon heads of [[tiamat|Tiamat]].

| # | Disciple | Notes |
|---|----------|-------|
| 1 | **[[gregory-white|Gregory White]]** | White dragon. Senior member of the [[crimson-assembly|Crimson Assembly]] in [[emond|Emond]]. Holds 1 of 3 [[vlakith|Vlakith]] dragon eggs. |
| 2 | **[[dolan-ward|Grim / Dolan Ward]]** | Crimson Assembly. In Emberstone. Building the dragon-aging machine. Holds 1 of 3 dragon eggs. |
| 3 | Unknown | — |
| 4 | **[[disciple-four|Unnamed]]** | Centuries old. Cultivates undead. Helmer aspired to this seat. |
| 5 | Unknown | — |

### Cell Operatives

- **[[helmer|Helmer]]** — Local cell leader in Emberstone, captured Session 15. Reported to Dolan; aspired to Disciple #4 seat.
- **Oderman** — Cell leader at Lord Therris's villa (Ch.1). Killed.
- **Derreck** — Oderman's lieutenant. Killed at Drageslottet.
- **Zuthan** — Drow commander at Drageslottet (Ch.1).

## Plans

- Gather all four elemental orbs at **Blade Garden** (once 2+ are collected)
- The **Earth Orb** was stolen from Dhildhor — Sufos's primary quest
- The **Water Orb** was defended (though Three Waters was at risk)
- An "eighth month" prophecy: a war between good and evil was predicted in a letter found on Oderman
- They sent a flying Drow ship + three fleets with ballistae to attack Three Waters from sea (defeated in Ch.1 finale)

## Notes

- Crimson Assembly archives have no records of the cult — deliberately erased (likely by Gregory White, who is now confirmed as Disciple #1 *and* a senior Crimson member)
- The cult is also operating in **Emberstone** — Helmer's cell ran logistics through [[carls-caravan-company|Carl's Caravan Company]]
- The Githyanki scepters (carved from Tiamat's teeth) are connected to her larger pact

## Doctrine (Session 15)

- **Tiamat thrives on chaos.** The cult uses **war** as a deliberate distraction strategy — generating noise to keep attention off the orb collection and Tiamat's resurrection.
- The current Kayrun/Thalia war is part of this strategy.

## The Vlakith–Tiamat Pact

- [[vlakith|Vlakith]] (Githyanki Queen) made a pact with [[tiamat|Tiamat]] long ago, while Tiamat still walked the material plane
- Tiamat foresaw she would be sealed in a prison
- The pact gave Githyanki access to red dragons → an alliance forged across millennia
- [[helmer|Helmer]] used this pact to acquire **three dragon eggs** from Vlakith
- One egg recovered by the party (Session 15); two remain (one with Dolan, one with Gregory White)

## The Dragon-Aging Machine

- [[dolan-ward|Dolan]] is building a machine that **rapidly ages dragons**
- Prior versions seen by the party: prototype in the northern mountain; another during the prequel game (a dragon castle)
- Long-term cult project; the missing catalyst has always been **time-magic**
- **Drow time-manipulation magic-tech** is the catalyst — used by Drow on spider eggs
- Dolan has been hunting it for years; finally established direct contact with the Drow holders
- The Drow / Gruun deal is the **delivery channel**
- Once operational: **dragons matured in days**, some large enough to lay new eggs (exponential)

## Plans

- Gather all four elemental orbs at **[[blade-garden|Blade Garden]]** (once 2+ collected)
- **Two orbs already taken:** Earth (stolen from Dhildhor) and Water (lost during/after Ch.1 finale)
- **Next target:** **Fire Orb** — held by Fire Ashari in [[vulca|Vulca]] / [[rath|Rath]] area
- **Final target:** **Air Orb** — location unknown
- Plan: take Rath/Vulca within weeks of getting the dragon-aging machine operational

## Allies / Enablers (not full members)

- **[[kynos-gruun|Kynos Gruun]]** — NOT a Disciple, but the Drow time-tech delivery enabler. Promised one of the dragons as personal payment.
- **The Drow of Kayrun** — possess the time-tech; trading it for war alliance with Gruun
