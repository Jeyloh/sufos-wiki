# Iron Ring

**Mercenary Company**

## Description

A mercenary company operating in and around Emberstone. An entire Iron Ring unit vanished from the city just before Kayryn ambassadors arrived to inspect them — this was connected to the Mind Flayer investigation.

## Relationship

Not directly engaged with. Associated with the broader political tensions in Emberstone. Previous "fuck-ups" with Iron Ring (and Embermines) are why Zeffel was sending the Seekers on the Underdark mission instead of them.

## Notes

- A whole unit vanished — believed to have been under the Mind Flayer's influence
- Their absence was timed just before Kayryn ambassadors arrived, suggesting deliberate manipulation
