# Githyanki

## Description

A militant planar race who ride red dragons into battle and serve their queen Vlaaketh. Their forces in this region are commanded by [[vharanis|General Vharanis]]. They maintain outposts in the deep Underdark, including the now-lost [[crech-mediin|Crech Mediin]].

## Notable Members

- **[[vharanis|Vharanis]]** — Supreme Commander, controls Thraxus the adult red dragon
- **[[ryth|Ryth]]** — Dragon rider, party's field contact
- **[[thraxus|Thraxus]]** — Vharanis's adult red dragon

## Relationship

Hostile at first (attacked at Zeffel's). Now in an uneasy alliance: the party retakes Crech Mediin, the Githyanki leave Emberstone alone. [[elris|Elris]] and [[helmer|Helmer]] are held as hostages.

## Notes

- Three dragon scepters exist, carved from Tiamat's wisdom teeth — pieces of an ancient pact
- The scepters may be tied to how the Githyanki broke free from the Mind Flayers' control long ago
- Their dragon scepter was stolen by the albino Mind Flayer (Svuss) working with [[kynos-gruun|Kynos Gruun]]
