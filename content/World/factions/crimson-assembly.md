# Crimson Assembly

**Wizard guild based in Emond. The Tharlian crown's intelligence arm (or part of it).**

## Description

A prestigious wizarding guild headquartered in Emond. Has connections to the Tharlian crown and aristocracy. Members include both loyalists and traitors.

## Notable Members

- **[[gregory-white|Gregory White]]** — senior member; both Dolan and Gordon work under him. **(Session 15: confirmed as a white dragon and Disciple #1 of the [[dragon-cult|Dragon Cult]] — the Assembly's leader IS the cult's leader.)**
- **[[dolan-ward|Dolan Ward]]** (Grim/Tiberius Glint) — traitor, allied with Dragon Cult. **(Session 15: confirmed as Disciple #2.)**
- **[[gordon-merek|Gordon Merek]]** — loyal agent, investigating Dolan; helped the Saints. *Investigating his own boss's lieutenant without knowing it.*
- **Ulrich Eben** (deceased) — former Archmage who wrote *Gods of War and Peace*
- **Stanley Thornewood** — Ambassador and Crimson member

## Notes

- The Crimson Assembly's archives contain **no records of Tiamat or her cult** — as if deliberately purged. Session 15: the purger is almost certainly Gregory White himself, head of both organizations.
- Gordon's earlier suspicion that the corruption "goes up to **Lord Gregory**" is now confirmed — Gregory White is the cult's #1 Disciple.
- Tiberius Glint was identified as a Crimson member by the ring on his finger (recognised by Murdoch)
