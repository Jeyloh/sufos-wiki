# Crimson Assembly

**Wizard guild based in Emond. The Tharlian crown's intelligence arm (or part of it).**

## Description

A prestigious wizarding guild headquartered in Emond. Has connections to the Tharlian crown and aristocracy. Members include both loyalists and traitors.

## Notable Members

- **[[dolan-ward|Dolan Ward]]** (Grim/Tiberius Glint) — traitor, allied with Dragon Cult
- **[[gordon-merek|Gordon Merek]]** — loyal agent, investigating Dolan; helped the Saints
- **Gregory White** — senior member; both Dolan and Gordon work under him
- **Ulrich Eben** (deceased) — former Archmage who wrote *Gods of War and Peace*
- **Stanley Thornewood** — Ambassador and Crimson member

## Notes

- The Crimson Assembly's archives contain **no records of Tiamat or her cult** — as if deliberately purged
- Someone inside the Assembly (or court) may be a traitor. Gordon suspects the connection goes up to **Lord Gregory** but is not certain.
- Tiberius Glint was identified as a Crimson member by the ring on his finger (recognised by Murdoch)
