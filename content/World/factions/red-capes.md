# Red Capes

**City Guard of Emberstone**

## Description

The official city guard of Emberstone, loyal to Copper Lord [[kynos-gruun|Kynos Gruun]]. Led by inspector [[ransell-bondiak|Ransell Bondiak]].

## Relationship

The party has caused significant guard casualties (directly and indirectly) in Emberstone. The Red Capes are suspicious of the party, though [[zeffel|Zeffel]]'s bribes have kept them from acting on it.

## Notes

- Several guards died as a result of the party's actions in Embermines
- Zeffel spent large sums bribing guards to look the other way
