# Copper Ring — Copper Lords

## Description

The ruling merchant class of Emberstone. Copper Lords control the city's trade, politics, and effectively its guard force. Membership in the Copper Ring means real power.

## Notable Members

- **[[zeffel|Zeffel]]** — Tiefling merchant, friendly to the party, plotting against Gruun
- **[[kynos-gruun|Kynos Gruun]]** — Current dominant Copper Lord; commands the Red Capes and Iron Ring; collaborating with the Mind Flayer
- **[[estian|Estian]]** — Wizard and Academy mentor; former teacher of [[dolan-ward|Dolan Ward]], current mentor of [[helmer|Helmer]]
- **[[onio|Onio]]** — Art dealer; runs Onio's Gallery; connection to Aradin's framing unclear

## Relationship

The party is effectively working for [[zeffel|Zeffel]] to undermine [[kynos-gruun|Kynos Gruun]]. Zeffel has dangled the possibility of the party gaining Copper Lord status.

## Notes

- Zeffel promised 20,000 gp for completing the Mind Flayer and Githyanki missions (both done)
- The party may be positioned to claim influence in Emberstone
