# Earth Ashari

## Description

One of the four Ashari orders dedicated to guarding the elemental planes. The Earth Ashari are Sufos's primary allegiance. They guard the boundary between the material world and the Elemental Plane of Earth.

## Notable Members

- **Sufos** — Cleric, active field agent
- **[[aradin|Aradin]]** — Field agent, undercover in Emberstone (missing)

## Relationship

Sufos's home organisation. He joined them in Dhildhor after his time as a dwarven rebel leader.

## Notes

- Base in Dhildhor
- [[azar|Azar]] (Fire Ashari) serves as Sufos's commanding contact for this mission
- [[aradin|Aradin]]'s disappearance is the inciting mission of Chapter 2
