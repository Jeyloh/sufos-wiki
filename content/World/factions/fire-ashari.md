# Fire Ashari

## Description

One of the four Ashari orders, guarding the boundary to the Elemental Plane of Fire. Sufos trained with the Fire Ashari at their blacksmithing lodge and achieved master smith status. His current commanding contact [[azar|Azar]] is a Fire Ashari leader.

## Notable Members

- **[[azar|Azar]]** — Sufos's superior, directs the mission via Sending spells

## Relationship

Secondary allegiance — Sufos trained and lived with them. Owes them professional respect and obedience to Azar's orders.

- Became a master blacksmith at their lodge
