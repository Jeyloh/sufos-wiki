# Chronicle — Sufos

*The journey of Sufos, Hill Dwarf Cleric of Moradin, member of the Earth Ashari.*

---

## Chapter 1 (Sessions 1–10)

*See Journal Chapter 1 and Full Journal med AI for complete recap.*

### Sessions 1–6: Three Waters

Sufos first encountered Murdoch Saints near the mountains of Wildmount while they were on a mission for the town council of **[[three-waters|Three Waters]]**. He helped them fight kenkuers and they recognised each other as heading in the same direction — against **[[dolan-ward|Dolan Ward]]** (Grim/Tiberius) and the Dragon Cult. Sufos had recognised the Dragon Cult emblem on a dagger at Lord Therris's villa — the same symbol associated with those who stole the **[[earth-orb|Earth Orb]]** from Dhildhor.

Key Chapter 1 events:
- Met the party; together raided **Lord Therris's villa** — killed cult leader **Oderman**, found prophecy letter ("the eighth month"). Lord Therris found dead in ritual, legless.
- Explored **Rumble Mines** / **Drageslottet** — encountered Drow (under Zuthan's command), kobold merchant M'Glukk, cultist officers (Derreck killed), a summoned devil-woman (freed — attacked enemies before leaving)
- Failed to defeat [[dolan-ward|Dolan Ward]] directly — forced to flee down a well. A black dragon flew up to Dolan.
- Met **[[gordon-merek|Gordon Merek]]** (Crimson Assembly agent), who teleported them to Emond
- In Emond: rescued Percy from court, fought a creature of pure darkness in the sewers, rescued **[[stanley-thornewood|Stanley Thornewood]]** from Boar (half-orc assassin)
- Sufos researched Tiamat at the library (found *Gods of War and Peace* by Ulrich Eben), learned about **Knights of the Eclipse** and dragon **Crulecs**
- Sufos paid wizard **Aghmar** to send coded message to Moltar → Moltar replied: contact "brothers at the water"; keep pursuing the Earth Orb
- Used Divination through Moradin to pinpoint all four elemental orbs
- Returned to Three Waters with teleport key from Stanley
- **Chapter 1 finale:** Defeated the Drow flying ship (commanded by **Leo's brother** — who was killed in the fight). Held off the combined invasion force.

### Chapter 1 Aftermath → Chapter 2 Start

After the failure to retrieve the Earth Orb and the defence of Three Waters, Sufos returned to Moltar in Dhildhor. Moltar ordered him to continue his pilgrimage to the Fire Ashari — be proactive, not reactive.

Sufos joined Fire Ashari, trained as blacksmith, earned master smith title, befriended **[[kai|Kai]]** and **[[solona|Solona]]** (Azar's lieutenants), helped recover the Fire Orb artifact, met **[[edna|Edna]]** in Three Waters, proposed marriage, began planning the wedding for Vulca.

- Sufos: receives urgent Sending from [[azar|Azar]]: Aradin missing in Emberstone
- Course changed: Emberstone, not Vulca

## Chapter 2 — Emberstone & the Underdark

### Session 1 — Day 10–11, Month 5
**Locations:** Airship (Air Paragons)

[[kaptein-cedric-krast|Kaptein Cedric Krast]] (half-orc with a curly moustache) commands the Air Paragons vessel. New crew member [[kael|Kael]] joins as scout, impresses with an acrobatic backflip. A letter is delivered to [[elris|Elris]] about a "Castor" who believes Elris is a prophet of the Chained God. The airship is named "Falsus' Vengeance" — though Elris's Int check fails and he writes "Falsus' Vegans" on the hull.

[[azar|Azar]] sends two Sending spells: Aradin has disappeared; a contact [[egdalen-warendin|Egdalén Warendin]] may have information, but she is not fully trusted.

Course changed to Emberstone. The wedding can wait.

The airship is attacked by a larger warship from **Kayryn**. [[kael|Kael]] proves more capable than expected. Elris kicks two officers overboard. The remaining crew surrenders. Two drow prisoners are taken.

---

### Session 2 — Day 11–17, Month 5
**Locations:** At sea / Airship, Outskirts of Emberstone

The party's ship is approached by a white-sailed vessel. A massive figure teleports aboard — [[overstyrsmann-lavant|Overstyrsmann Lavant]], a giant-like ship wizard. He represents **Kaptein Anlow** of the Tharlian ship *The Strider*, who wishes to board and inspect the captured ship *Utherath*. [[captain-anlow|Captain Anlow]] is a stern figure who offers 1750 gp immediately, with 7250 gp pending court processing.

[[azar|Azar]] sends a second message: keep a low profile. Aradin knew about a Tiamat cult cell in Emberstone before he disappeared.

A desert caravan is spotted being attacked by ogres, including a distinctive blue-tinged one. The party engages and defeats them all, though an **Ogre Mage** causes trouble. The grateful caravan workers travel with the party; their leader [[arathan|Arathan]] works for [[zeffel|Zeffel]], a powerful Tiefling merchant in Emberstone's **Copper Ring**. Silks are offered as thanks.

Emberstone comes into view: three red cliffs cradling an oasis, buildings carved into the rock, great walls encircling the city.

---

### Session 3 — Day 17–18, Month 5
**Locations:** Emberstone, Zeffel's establishment

The Saints arrive in Emberstone and are met by [[ransell-bondiak|Ransell Bondiak]], inspector of the **Red Capes**, loyal to Copper Lord [[kynos-gruun|Kynos Gruun]].

[[cheereen|Cheereen]], a paladin of Bahamut, is investigating vandalism to the statues of gods outside the Temple of Joint Worship.

At [[zeffel|Zeffel]]'s establishment, the party meets him and [[helmer|Helmer]], an archaeologist and acquaintance of [[estian|Estian]]. The meeting is interrupted by four Gith warriors attacking. Zeffel and Helmer join the fight; Helmer attempts to calm the Gith in their own language. A young red dragon smashes through the windows. Another red dragon outside is massacred by an adult Brass Dragon. The party takes control of the young dragon and secures its promise of a future favour before releasing it.

**LEVEL 9!**

Zeffel rewards 5000 gp and promises 20,000 more for:
1. Finding and killing a Mind Flayer hiding in the city
2. Travelling into the Underdark and negotiating with the Githyanki — presenting the Mind Flayer's head and returning the swords of the fallen Gith

Sufos notices Murdoch's shadow flickering strangely.

At [[egdalen-warendin|Egdalén]]'s, the party trades information. They learn: Dolan Ward/Grim studied at the Amathyst Academy; [[aradin|Aradin]] the Ashari agent is imprisoned in **Vulture's Vault** (the Embermines prison), framed for a theft at Onyo's gallery. Deck of Many Things is drawn with mixed results — Sufos is unlucky, gaining two difficult cards including one that hexes him (−2 to all stats).

The party infiltrates the prison and speaks briefly with Aradin with a guard present. Using Thieves' Cant, Aradin hints at a collapsed cell near a Embermines cave-in.

Leo kills the gate guard before he finishes ringing the alarm. The party charges into the Embermines.

---

### Session 4 — Day 18–19, Month 5
**Locations:** Embermines, Underdark

Inside the mine, Sufos animates **stone warriors** to delay pursuing soldiers. Deep in a large cavern, three devil-like figures appear. The largest — a glaskie devil with wings and horns — claims to serve **Zariel**, ruler of Avernus (the first layer of Hell). He reveals that Murdoch carries an ancient shadow demon named **[[orgaz|Orgaz]]** and offers to free him from it without Murdoch losing his powers. Murdoch denies the demon's existence. The devil ignites the torches and forces Orgaz into view as a great shadow behind Murdoch, then disappears.

The situation grows tense. Murdoch tries to Banish Orgaz but accidentally targets himself as the same entity. Orgaz charms Murdoch and retreats back into his shadow. Murdoch refuses the deal.

Sufos receives a **vision from Moradin** — clearly disappointed in some of the party's choices. Sufos is temporarily stripped of the **diamond tooth**, but earns it back during the session.

Five soldiers close in; the party takes them out. Orgaz appears to drain the youngest soldier's soul as he dies.

The party explores old Githyanki ruins in the mine and battles three **Umberhulks**. They locate a collapsed chamber with a crack into the prison and a chasm into the Underdark. Aradin's cell appears empty — he has been moved. They descend into the Underdark.

While resting, Duergars demand a bribe to leave them alone. They pay and rest. Elris scouts and is spotted by a prison guard. The party retreats deeper, encountering a giant cavern with a Duergar camp (avoided) and a bone-filled grotto where armoured humanoids warn them away from going further.

---

### Session 5 — Day 19, Month 5
**Locations:** Embermines exit, Emberstone

The party returns from the chasm and encounters guards patching the mine wall. A Zeffel-bought guard vouches for them. The party learns Zeffel is furious about the number of dead guards; he has been spending heavily in bribes. [[ransell-bondiak|Ransell Bondiak]] and the halfling inspector ask pointed questions about their timing.

At the slave market, the party buys free three gnomes working for **Mungo Murdoch**, Murdoch's father. Leo cannot resist stealing from a slaver.

The party visits **Afterlife** casino — Murdoch wins some gold.

Investigating the Mind Flayer: they learn that an Iron Ring mercenary unit vanished from the city just before ambassadors from Kayryn arrived to inspect them. They locate the barracks.

The barracks are full of brain-dead soldiers walking on autopilot and loaded with traps. An albino Mind Flayer appears, begins to fight, then teleports away just before the party corners it.

**LEVEL 10**

### Session 6 — Day 19–20, Month 5
**Locations:** Emberstone barracks, Zeffel's

The party loots the barracks and finds a journal written in Deep Speech / Undercommon. [[leo|Leo]] translates:

- Part 1: A ritual using an amulet that extends the life of a group of exiled Illithids. Duergars were enslaved to forge it, then killed, their life-force bound to it.
- Part 2: A successful plot to steal the **dragon scepter** from the Githyanki outpost beneath Emberstone: **Crech Mediin**. The scepter has three properties: locate dragons (especially red); summon nearby ones; dominate them. Three such scepters exist, carved from Tiamat's wisdom teeth — part of an ancient pact that helped the Githyanki break free from the Illithids.
- Additional: The Mind Flayer (**Svuss**) has not yet been able to use the scepter — an enchantment prevents non-Githyanki from using it. [[kynos-gruun|Kynos Gruun]] is implicated as a collaborator.

Also looted: magical dagger, gold (split with Sufos), spellbook, scroll (Wither and Bloom), **Gloves of Missile Snaring**.

Sufos prays at the temple.

The party visits [[zeffel|Zeffel]] and encounters [[kynos-gruun|Kynos Gruun]], who reminds them of their debt and mentions the arena. Several party members get magical tattoos en route.

That night, Leo wakes in agony — the Mind Flayer remotely attacking him, demanding his journal back. The party kills it and recovers the Mind Flayer's head, the scepter, and the amulet.

They decide to approach the Githyanki directly.

Heading into the Underdark toward the lava-lit deep, they encounter Gith warriors who are not interested in talking. A brutal fight breaks out — Sufos nearly falls. Several Gith are hurled into lava by Murdoch's magic. An adult red dragon and rider emerge from the darkness.

---

### Session 7 (Player: Session 12) — Day 20–21, Month 5
**Locations:** Underdark deep, Crystal Corridor, Fungal Garden, approach to Crech Mediin

The party meets **[[vharanis|General Vharanis]]** ("Rhanis" in Sufos's notes) (Githyanki Supreme Commander) and his adult red dragon **[[thraxus|Thraxus]]**. They buy an audience by presenting the Mind Flayer's head. [[elris|Elris]] and [[helmer|Helmer]] are kept behind as insurance for the party's success.

An alliance is struck:
- The party receives the **dragon scepter** (Vharanis visibly relaxes — control of his dragon restored)
- Vharanis will leave Emberstone alone
- The party gets the dragon rider **[[ryth|Ryth]]** ("Rath") as guide and contact
- Contact Ryth when Crech Mediin leadership is eliminated

Sufos uses **Commune** — asking Moradin three questions:
1. *Will the demon in Murdoch attack again?* — **YES**
2. *Is it Tiamat the Drow are summoning in Crech Mediin?* — **NO** (it is Lolth / Lady Eovah)
3. *Can I be forgiven?* — **Yes (tentative)**

Sufos then uses **Divine Intervention** — asking Moradin for strength and to lift his curse. Moradin's response: a hint — *"Use adamantine and holy power to destroy the enemy's stones. That is the most effective way."*

Through the **Crystal Corridor**: hostile Duergars in ambush — defeated.

At the **Fungal Garden**: [[ryth|Ryth]]'s old informant [[jim-jam|Jim Jam]] (Deep Gnome) has sided with the new drow leadership. Attacks with druids and two stone elementals. Defeated; taken prisoner as guide. A chest found in a cave; Jim Jam unlocks it. Party acquires **6 scrolls**: Silence, Fly, Disguise Self, Fireball, Detect Magic, Greater Restoration.

Party dodges a giant worm tunnel on the way to the Black Lake. Ryth kicks Jim Jam into the lake (creature with tentacles detected by Commune) — party escapes. Climbs rope ladder to Crech Mediin's back entrance. Long rest at the top.

---

### Session 8 (Player: Session 13) — Day 22, Month 5
**Locations:** Crech Mediin / House of Webs — interior and temple

The party sneaks into Crech Mediin but is quickly spotted by **Drow Elites**. They move toward the temple. Outside the temple entrance: many Drow and a priest gathered. They flee inside when they sense the party. The party loots what it can, then enters the temple.

Inside: layers of massive cobwebs, the priest, and Drow being sent as sacrifices.

**The Lolth Aspect Fight**
Three stones in three separate portals must be destroyed to prevent the resurrection of **[[lady-eowa|Lady Eovah]]** as a Lolth aspect. Leo rushes the third portal for the third stone; Murdoch destroys the second stone. But the aspect of Lolth wakes before all three are destroyed — becomes a significant enemy.

- Leo falls; Sufos heals him
- Sufos and Leo fall; Murdoch draws attention
- **[[elris|Elris]] arrives** — gives the party 2 healing potions to get back up
- [[ryth|Ryth]] enters and helps finish the aspect

The aspect is defeated narrowly. **[[lady-eowa|Lady Eovah]]** is slain.

**Ryth's betrayal**: He turns on the party immediately after, threatening them for information about **"Garax"** — his red dragon the party had subdued previously. The party tells him what they know.

**Lolth's manifestation**: A magical darkness falls over the room. [[lolth|Lolth]] enters the minds of everyone *except Ryth* (who carries an item protecting him). Sufos attempts Divine Intervention — it fails. Lolth turns on Sufos specifically:

> *"Shall you be holy?! The party you run with are murderers, losers, betrayers, and evil."*

The party bleeds from eyes and ears. They come to back in the original room where they fought the aspect. Session ends.
